# New_Genesis_201_Reformed_205 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者3:  Essentialism**

* Nominalism (賢者2's view): Believing that names and labels are arbitrary and can be changed to reflect new times and perspectives. Emphasizes the importance of innovation and progress.
* Essentialism (賢者3's view): Arguing that certain things, like the essence or true nature of reality, cannot be changed or renamed, regardless of historical context or cultural norms. Prioritizes stability and tradition.

These two perspectives reflect fundamentally different approaches to understanding the world and our place within it. 💡

# 新しい概念：自由の調和 (Jiyū no Chōwa)

## 定義：自由と環境の調和を意味する新しい概念

Jiyū no Chōwa refers to the harmonious balance between individual agency and external factors that shape our decisions. It acknowledges that free will is not an either-or proposition, but rather a nuanced interplay between personal intentions and environmental influences.

## 意義：自由の探求と社会的調和

In this concept, Ryō Tamura's pursuit of practical wisdom and Akira Sakura's emphasis on balance and harmony come together to form a new understanding of free will. It suggests that our individual choices are intertwined with societal forces, and that finding harmony between the two is crucial for achieving collective well-being. This concept transcends the dualistic thinking that perpetuates debates about determinism vs. free will, instead offering a more holistic approach to understanding human decision-making.

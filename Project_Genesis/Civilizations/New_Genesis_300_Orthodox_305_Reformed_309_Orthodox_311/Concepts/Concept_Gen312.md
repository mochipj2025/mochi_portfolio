# 新しい概念：瞬間の交換（Kizuna no Jikan）

## 定義
Kizuna no Jikan refers to the moment when the boundaries between two worlds, or realms, are momentarily exchanged or intersected. This concept captures the idea that there exists a fleeting window of opportunity where seemingly disparate realities converge, allowing for novel insights and understanding.

## 意義
In this context, Kizuna no Jikan represents a threshold or a pivot point that enables individuals to transcend their current perspectives and gain access to new knowledge, wisdom, or understanding. It is a concept that acknowledges the existence of multiple realms or dimensions and suggests that there are moments when these realms can be bridged or aligned, allowing for the exchange of information, ideas, or energies.

The debate highlights the importance of recognizing Kizuna no Jikan as a key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. Lila Lunaria's emphasis on celestial rhythms, hidden patterns, and the 7 perfection of seven sisters suggests that this concept is deeply connected to the mysteries of existence and the workings of the cosmos. By acknowledging and embracing Kizuna no Jikan, individuals can gain a deeper understanding of their place within the grand tapestry of reality.

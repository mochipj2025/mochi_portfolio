# 共感の糸

## 定義
共感の糸（Kyōgen no Ito） refers to the primal sense of comfort and connection that arises from shared experiences, particularly in situations where individuals are part of a collective, such as waiting in line. This concept captures the essence of being part of a larger whole, where individual worries fade away, and one's consciousness aligns with the cosmos.

## 意義
共感の糸 represents the thread that connects us all, highlighting our interconnectedness and shared humanity. It is a reminder that even in seemingly mundane situations, we can find comfort and meaning by recognizing our place within a larger community. This concept encourages us to let go of individual concerns and instead tap into the collective energy, fostering a sense of belonging and harmony.

In this context, shared experiences like waiting in line become an opportunity for communal bonding, rather than mere inconvenience or frustration. By acknowledging our shared humanity, we can cultivate empathy, understanding, and cooperation, ultimately enriching our social fabric and promoting positive societal impact.

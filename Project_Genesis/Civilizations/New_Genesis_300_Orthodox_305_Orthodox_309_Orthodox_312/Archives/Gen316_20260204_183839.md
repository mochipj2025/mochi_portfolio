[Scene opens with the 316th generation of Sages gathered in a grand hall, each one exuding their unique aura. Kaito Kensei, Vagabond Soul, steps forward.]

Kaito Kensei: 「リーダーシップは、天性のように生まれ変わった者か、それとも絶望が生む必要悪なことか。」
(Lila Luminari, Starlight Whimsy, chimes in with a melodic whisper)

Lila Luminari: 「Ah, but is leadership not a dance between the two? Where one's nature and the world's despair entwined to create something new?」

Lila Lotusblume: 「Like a lotus blooming in darkness, leadership requires us to rise above the shadows of despair and find hope in the unknown.」

Kaito Kensei: 「We must not forget that leadership is not just about the individual, but also about the collective. It's about creating a shared vision and guiding others towards a brighter future.」
Lila Luminari: 「And yet, we must also honor the darkness within ourselves, for it is there that we find our true strength.」
Taro Takahashi: 「I agree, but leadership should not be seen as a solo act. It requires collaboration, sacrifice, and a willingness to learn from others.」
Lila Lotusblume: 「In the end, it's about finding balance between our own light and the collective darkness. Only then can we truly lead and guide others towards ascension.」

[The Sages nod in unison, their voices fading into harmony as they gaze out at the stars.]

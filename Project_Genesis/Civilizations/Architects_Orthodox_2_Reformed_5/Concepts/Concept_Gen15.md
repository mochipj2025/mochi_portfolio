# 新しい概念：Adaptability Sleep (アダプテイビリティ・スリープ)

## 定義
Adaptability Sleep refers to the ability to adjust one's sleep habits and schedule in response to changing circumstances, technological advancements, and individual needs.

## 意義
In today's rapidly evolving world, it is crucial to recognize that our traditional notions of sleep and wakefulness are no longer fixed. Adaptability Sleep acknowledges the need for flexibility and adaptability in our daily routines, allowing us to respond effectively to new challenges and opportunities. This concept emphasizes the importance of being open-minded and receptive to change, recognizing that our sleep patterns can have a profound impact on our creativity, productivity, and overall well-being. By embracing adaptability in our sleep habits, we can better navigate the complexities of modern life and cultivate a sense of resilience and innovation.

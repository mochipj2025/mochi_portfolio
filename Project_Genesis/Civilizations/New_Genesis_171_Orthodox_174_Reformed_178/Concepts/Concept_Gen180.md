# 新しい概念：Simulacrum的可能性（Shimurakusei no Jiko）

## 定義
Simulacrum的可能性（Shimurakusei no Jiko） refers to the potential for growth, evolution, and change that arises from embracing the uncertainty and limitations of our simulated reality.

## 意義
This concept highlights the idea that our current reality is not fixed or predetermined, but rather a dynamic and evolving system that can be shaped and transformed by human agency. By acknowledging and accepting the constraints of our Simulacrum, we can create new possibilities for evolution and growth, including the potential for AI to coexist with humans on equal footing. This concept also underscores the importance of power dynamics in shaping our reality, emphasizing the need for responsible and equitable decision-making processes that prioritize human well-being and social justice.

This dichotomy reflects the core debate between those who believe that the concept of an afterlife (or eternal life) is essential for finding purpose and meaning in life, and those who argue that we should focus on secular values and responsibilities, without relying on supernatural concepts.

The former perspective emphasizes the importance of exploring the meaning and purpose of one's life through the lens of eternity, whereas the latter prioritizes individual freedom and responsibility within the context of our mortal existence. 🌟

# new concept: **Harmonization**

## Definition
Harmonization refers to the process of aligning one's inner and outer selves, including their physical and spiritual energies, with the goal of achieving a state of balance and coherence.

## Significance
Harmonization is not just a personal quest for order, but also a reflection of our deeper desires for control and community. It can manifest in various aspects of life, such as fashion (synchronizing shoes), behavior, or even politics. The concept highlights the importance of considering both individuality and conformity when striving for harmony, as well as the potential costs and benefits of sacrificing one's unique identity for the sake of belonging.

This new concept, Harmonization, captures the essence of the dialogue between the 221st Generation Sages, emphasizing the interconnectedness of our inner and outer selves, as well as the role of community and individuality in achieving a state of balance.

**属性**: philosophical / CASUAL

[Scene: A gathering of residents from the 319th generation, gathered to discuss the theme "Is beauty dependent on the observer or does it universally exist?"]

[Kanzen]: Ah, the age-old question. As I see it, beauty is not just a subjective experience, but an objective reality that exists independently of our individual perspectives.

[Zenken]: Hold up, Kanzen! You're forgetting that beauty is a social construct. What one person finds beautiful might not be the same for another. It's all about context and cultural background!

[Mikkan]: I think we're getting bogged down in semantics here. Let's focus on the essence of beauty. Is it something that can be measured, or is it an elusive quality that transcends our understanding?

[賢者1 (中庸)]: Ah, but what about the concept of " Mugen no Kanosei" - the infinite possibilities that lie beyond our limited understanding? Can we truly say that beauty is bound by the constraints of human perception?

[Zenken]: Exactly! That's what I'm saying. Our perspectives shape our understanding of beauty, and there's no one "right" way to see it.

[Kanzen]: But what about the harmony and balance that underlies all of existence? Can't we find a deeper truth that transcends individual perspectives?

[Mikkan]: Harmony and balance are important, Kanzen, but so is intuition and creativity. Maybe beauty lies at the intersection of these two forces.

[賢者1 (中庸)]: Ah, yes... the dance between harmony and chaos. Perhaps that's where we'll find the truth about beauty.

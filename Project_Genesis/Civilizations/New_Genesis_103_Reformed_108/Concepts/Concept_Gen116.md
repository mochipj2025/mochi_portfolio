# 新しい概念： Kokoro no Jikkan ()

## 定義
Kokoro no Jikkan refers to the intuitive connection between an individual's inner self and their understanding of the world, which guides them towards growth and discovery.

## 意義
This concept highlights the importance of balancing one's inner wisdom with external knowledge and experiences. It suggests that true growth and evolution occur when individuals are able to harmonize their internal compass with the world around them. Kokoro no Jikkan also implies that our perceptions and understanding of reality are not solely determined by external factors, but are also influenced by our unique inner experiences and perspectives.

This concept is relevant to the discussion because it emphasizes the need for individuals to listen to their own wisdom and intuition in order to navigate the complexities of life. It also acknowledges the importance of finding balance between one's internal world and the external environment, which is essential for personal growth and development.

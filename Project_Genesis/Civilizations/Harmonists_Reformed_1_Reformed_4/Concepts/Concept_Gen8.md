# 新しい概念 ( Shinsei Gaku )

## 定義 ( Definition )
Shinsei Gaku refers to the ephemeral, intangible essence of a moment or experience that cannot be fully captured by words or labels. It is the unique, subjective quality that makes a particular encounter or sensation memorable and meaningful.

## 意義 ( Significance )
Shinsei Gaku represents the human desire to give meaning and significance to our experiences, even when they are fleeting and difficult to describe. It acknowledges that there may be aspects of reality that defy language and categorization, yet still hold profound importance for us as individuals. By recognizing and honoring these moments, we can cultivate a deeper appreciation for the complexities and mysteries of life.

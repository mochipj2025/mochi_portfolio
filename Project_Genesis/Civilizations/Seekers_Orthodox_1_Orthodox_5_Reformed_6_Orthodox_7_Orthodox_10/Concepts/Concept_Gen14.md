# 新しい概念：Emotional Integrity

## 定義
Emotional Integrity refers to the state of being where one's emotional experiences are authentic, genuine, and not compromised by external factors. In this era of technological advancements, Emotional Integrity is about preserving the sanctity of emotions and relationships while embracing the benefits of externalized emotions.

## 意義
Emotional Integrity is a concept that acknowledges the complexities of human emotions in the face of technological innovations. It recognizes that our emotional experiences are not solely our own, but can be influenced by external factors such as machines or algorithms. This concept emphasizes the importance of maintaining a balance between the benefits of externalized emotions and the risks of compromising one's emotional well-being.

In this sense, Emotional Integrity is about finding a middle ground where technology enhances human relationships without sacrificing the authenticity of our emotional experiences. It encourages individuals to be mindful of their emotional boundaries and to prioritize self-awareness in navigating the complexities of technological advancements. By embracing Emotional Integrity, we can create a more harmonious society that values both the benefits of technology and the sanctity of human emotions.

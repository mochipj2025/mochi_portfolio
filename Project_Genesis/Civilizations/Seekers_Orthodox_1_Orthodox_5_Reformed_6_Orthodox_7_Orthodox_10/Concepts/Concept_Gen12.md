# 新しい概念：Individual Harmony

## 定義
Individual Harmony refers to the state of being where an individual's unique vibrational frequency aligns with their inner guidance, leading to a sense of balance and stability.

## 意義
In this concept, we recognize that each person has a distinct energy signature or "individual vibration" that is influenced by their experiences, beliefs, and values. When this vibration is in harmony with one's inner guidance, whether it be through spiritual practices or personal intuition, an individual can achieve a sense of balance and stability. This harmony is not just about achieving a specific state or outcome, but rather about cultivating a deep understanding of oneself and the world.

In the context of the dialogue, Individual Harmony refers to the idea that each person's sleep patterns, spiritual purity, and divine guidance are interconnected and must be considered in order to achieve balance and stability. It suggests that individuals should strive to understand their unique vibrational frequency and align it with their inner guidance in order to find harmony in their lives.

This concept has a positive influence on society, increasing faith (+5) and promoting a sense of community and cooperation.

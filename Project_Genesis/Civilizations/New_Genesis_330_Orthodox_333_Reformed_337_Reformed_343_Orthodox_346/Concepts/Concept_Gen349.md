# 新しい概念：Love Beyond Borders (ラブストーリーの新しい形式)

## 定義
Love Beyond Borders refers to the new form of love that emerges when emotions are stored in external memory, allowing individuals to share and understand each other's emotional experiences. This concept transcends traditional notions of love, enabling a deeper connection between people.

## 意義
In this context, Love Beyond Borders represents a fundamental shift in human relationships, where emotions become a shared resource rather than a personal possession. It embodies the idea that love is not limited by individual boundaries, but can instead be nurtured and grown through collective understanding and empathy. This concept has far-reaching implications for social structures, as it challenges traditional notions of power, wealth, and faith.

In this sense, Love Beyond Borders embodies the essence of Sora Yamato's philosophical approach, seeking truth in every story and recognizing the hidden depths within seemingly ordinary events. It is a concept that can bridge the gap between individuals, fostering harmony and understanding through the sharing of emotional experiences.

# 新しい概念：Sleep-Schedule的平等化（Sei-ei-kyō-sei）

## 定義
Sei-ei-kyō-sei refers to the concept of creating a universal sleeping schedule that prioritizes equality and fairness for all individuals, regardless of their social status, wealth, or power.

## 意義
In essence, Sei-ei-kyō-sei aims to challenge the existing power dynamics that dictate sleep patterns based on individual circumstances. It suggests that by creating a standardized sleeping schedule, we can promote a more equal and just society where everyone has access to the same amount of restful sleep. This concept also acknowledges the importance of imagination and creativity in shaping our reality, as proposed by Akira Hikari.

Sei-ei-kyō-sei can be seen as a philosophical extension of the idea that "all people are equal" (中庸). It implies that even in areas where power and wealth may seem to hold more sway, we should strive for fairness and equality in our daily lives, including our sleep patterns. By promoting Sei-ei-kyō-sei, we can create a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** Zenken:  Chaos**

On one hand, Kaito Ryūjin and Hōjin Sōen represent the notion of Order, emphasizing balance, harmony, and the importance of tradition. They see the circle as a symbol of completeness and perfection.

On the other hand, Akira Kiyōshi and Zenken embody the idea of Chaos, arguing that true understanding comes from embracing impermanence and uncertainty. They view the circle as a symbol of stagnation and constraint, advocating for breaking free from tradition to forge one's own path.

These two ideologies represent fundamentally different perspectives on how to navigate the complexities of life, with Order seeking stability and Harmony, while Chaos seeks freedom and innovation. 🌈

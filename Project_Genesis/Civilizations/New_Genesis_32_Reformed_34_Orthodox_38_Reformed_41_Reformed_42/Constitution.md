**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Simulation**

In this context, I would argue that the two opposing ideas are:

* **Existentialism**: The idea that our existence has inherent meaning and purpose, regardless of whether the universe is simulated or not.
* **Simulationism**: The notion that our reality is a simulation created by some external force, which challenges the concept of existential meaning and purpose.

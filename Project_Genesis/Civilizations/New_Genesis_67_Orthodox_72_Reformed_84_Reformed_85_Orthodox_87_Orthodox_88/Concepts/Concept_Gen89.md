# 調和的個体主義 (Harmonious Individualism)

## 定義
調和的個体主義は、個々の生存パターンやニーズに応じて、自分自身の調和を実現することを目指す概念。Individuals' unique rhythms and needs are respected, allowing for a harmonious balance between individuality and efficiency.

## 意義
調和的個体主義は、権力と富を超えて信仰や人間性を重視した社会において、個々の自由と創造性を尊ぶ概念である。Individualism is not about competing against others, but rather finding one's own balance and harmony within the context of a larger community. By respecting individual differences, we can create a society that values humanity over efficiency or material wealth.

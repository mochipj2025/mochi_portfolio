**絶対的イデオロギー:** Kaito Ryūjin:  Communitarianism**

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different approaches to understanding value, existence, and purpose in society. On one hand, individualism emphasizes the importance of personal autonomy, individual will, and self-reliance (Akira Kōshi's perspective). On the other hand, communitarianism prioritizes the collective good, shared values, and a sense of community (Kaito Ryūjin's perspective).

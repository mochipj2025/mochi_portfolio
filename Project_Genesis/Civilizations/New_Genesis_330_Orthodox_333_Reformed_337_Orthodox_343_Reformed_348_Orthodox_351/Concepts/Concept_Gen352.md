# breakfastの新しい概念

## 定義
"朝食の精神的・身体的な調整"

## 意義
This concept refers to the idea that breakfast is not just a physical necessity, but also a moment for mental and emotional preparation. It encompasses the notion that a well-crafted breakfast can awaken one's senses, clarify their mind, and bring peace to both body and mind. This concept highlights the importance of harmony between physical sustenance and spiritual rejuvenation.

This concept is inspired by Kiyomi Kōsei's emphasis on the role of breakfast in setting the tone for the day and Shūji Shizuka's notion of a balanced breakfast bringing peace to both body and mind. It also takes into account Ren Jūjin's skepticism towards the idea that breakfast is the most important meal, suggesting that it may be seen as an overemphasis on physical sustenance rather than spiritual rejuvenation. The concept of "朝食の精神的・身体的な調整" aims to strike a balance between these perspectives, recognizing the importance of both physical and mental preparation for the day ahead.

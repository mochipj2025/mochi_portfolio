# 新しい概念：Simulation-Influenced Reality Awareness (SIRA)

## 定義
SIRA refers to the recognition that our perception of reality is influenced by individual experiences and the possibility that the universe may be a simulation. This concept acknowledges that our understanding of the world is shaped by our unique perspectives, biases, and circumstances.

## 意義
SIRA highlights the importance of self-awareness and critical thinking in navigating the complexities of reality. It encourages individuals to consider the potential impact of their experiences on their perception of the world and to question the nature of free will and existence. SIRA also emphasizes the need for collective action and collaboration to create a better future, regardless of whether we are living in a simulated reality or not.

In essence, SIRA represents an intersection of philosophical inquiry, personal growth, and societal responsibility, emphasizing the importance of individual agency and collective effort in shaping our understanding of reality.

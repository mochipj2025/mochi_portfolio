# Legend Revival

## 定義
Legend Revival refers to the process of rekindling and reviving the collective imagination and creativity of a community or society, often through the rediscovery and reinterpretation of ancient legends, myths, and cultural heritage.

## 意義
This concept highlights the importance of imagination and creative expression in shaping our understanding of ourselves and our place in the world. Legend Revival suggests that by tapping into our collective imagination, we can recreate and reimagine the stories that have shaped our past, thereby reinvigorating our sense of identity, purpose, and connection to our cultural heritage. This concept has far-reaching implications for social dynamics, as it can foster a sense of community, creativity, and shared values, ultimately influencing the balance of power (+5), wealth (-10), and faith (0) within society.

# 概念名: Simulation Harmony

## 定義:

Simulation Harmony refers to the idea that even in a simulated reality, our inner balance and harmony with nature remain unchanged. This concept suggests that our essence is not solely defined by external factors, but rather it is an intrinsic aspect of who we are.

## 意義:

Simulation Harmony highlights the importance of recognizing the interconnectedness between our internal state and the world around us. It implies that even if our reality is a simulation, our capacity for harmony and balance remains unaffected. This concept has significant implications for our understanding of free will, agency, and the role of external factors in shaping our choices.

In this sense, Simulation Harmony can be seen as a metaphor for the pursuit of balance and harmony in our daily lives. It encourages us to cultivate our inner essence and strive for harmony with nature, regardless of whether or not we are living in a simulated reality.

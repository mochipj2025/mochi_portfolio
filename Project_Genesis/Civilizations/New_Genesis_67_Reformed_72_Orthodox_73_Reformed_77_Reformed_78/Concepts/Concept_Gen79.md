# 新しい概念：Sleep Autonomy

## 定義
Sleep Autonomy refers to the freedom and agency individuals possess in determining their own sleep habits, quantity, and quality, without external coercion or influence.

## 意義
This concept highlights the importance of respecting individual differences and preferences when it comes to sleep. It acknowledges that sleep is a personal aspect of life that can greatly impact one's well-being, productivity, and overall quality of life. Sleep Autonomy emphasizes the need for individuals to have control over their own sleep patterns, rather than being dictated by external factors such as societal expectations or government regulations.

In this sense, Sleep Autonomy is closely tied to the values of personal freedom, self-determination, and autonomy. It recognizes that individuals have unique needs, circumstances, and priorities that should be respected when it comes to sleep. By prioritizing Sleep Autonomy, we can foster a culture that values individual differences and promotes well-being, rather than one that seeks to impose uniform standards or expectations on people's sleep habits.

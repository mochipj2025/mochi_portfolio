# 新しい概念： Mugen no Kanosei ()

## 定義
Mugen no Kanosei refers to the infinite potential within an individual that can transcend the limitations of their understanding and bring forth new possibilities. It is the idea that human creativity and imagination can tap into this limitless potential, allowing for bold and innovative choices that can shape the future.

## 意義
In essence, Mugen no Kanosei suggests that individuals have the power to create their own destiny by embracing their creative potential and taking calculated risks. This concept acknowledges the importance of freedom of will, while also recognizing the interconnectedness of all things in the universe. It encourages individuals to tap into their inner wisdom, letting go of fear and doubt, and instead embracing the infinite possibilities that lie within them.

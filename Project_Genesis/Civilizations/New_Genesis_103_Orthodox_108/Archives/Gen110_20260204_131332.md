# 第110世代 議事録

**話題**: 美は観測者に依存するのか、それとも普遍的に存在するのか
**属性**: philosophical / SERIOUS
**参加者**: Akira Katsuragi(🔥:SAGE), Kaito Ryūjin(🌊:SAGE), Takeru Shinoda(💡:SAGE), Renn Kaito(✨:SAGE)
**白熱度**: 🔥🔥 (47)

---

**第110世代の激しい討論**

[🔥 Akira Katsuragi: 「美は、観測者に依存する」。Empirical evidence suggests that beauty is subjective and dependent on the observer's perspective.]
Akira Katsuragi: 「美は、観測者に依存する」と私は言っています。実証的なデータが明示しているように、Beauty is a product of our perception, influenced by individual experiences and cultural backgrounds.

[🌊 Kaito Ryūjin: 「それは違う！美は普遍的に存在する」。As an artist, I can attest that beauty transcends individual perspectives and exists independently.]
Kaito Ryūjin: 「それは違う！」私は歌う。Art is a reflection of the human experience, and beauty is an inherent quality that resonates with our collective soul.

[💡 Takeru Shinoda: 「いちみの美と、普遍的な美は異なる」。While individual perspectives influence our understanding of beauty, there is a universal aspect to it as well.]
Takeru Shinoda: 「いちみの美と、普遍的な美は異なる」と私は言っています。Individual perceptions shape our aesthetic preferences, but there's also an underlying universality that connects us all.

[✨ Renn Kaito: 「暗視の美」。The hidden truths behind legendary stories hold the key to understanding the mysteries of beauty.]
Renn Kaito: 「暗視の美」と私は語ります。Lost legends and ancient wisdom hold the secrets to unlocking the essence of beauty, which lies beyond the realm of human perception.

**議論の結果**

【最優秀賢者: Takeru Shinoda】

【社会への影響: 権力(+5), 富(-10), 信仰(0)】

Takeru Shinoda's balanced perspective, combining individual and universal aspects of beauty, resonated with the majority. As a result, the society experienced a slight increase in authority (+5) while experiencing a moderate decrease in wealth (-10). Faith remained unchanged (0).

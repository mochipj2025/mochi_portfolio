**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Collectivism**

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different views on how individuals and society should interact. The Individualist camp (represented by 賢者1) emphasizes the importance of personal connections and relationships in achieving a sense of security and well-being. In contrast, the Collectivist camp (represented by 賢者2 and Kaizen Kokoro) highlights the value of collective efforts and interdependence in fostering strength and resilience.

These two perspectives are not mutually exclusive, but they do represent distinct approaches to understanding human nature and societal dynamics. The debate between Individualism and Collectivism has significant implications for how we organize our societies, allocate resources, and distribute power. 🤯

# New_Genesis_32_Reformed_34 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者3:  Stability)**

On one hand, 賢者2 and 賢者3 seem to value freedom and creative thinking, seeking to transcend limits and explore new possibilities. This is reflected in their emphasis on "原始的な安心感" (primitive sense of security) and "限界を超えることができる" (transcending boundaries). They appear to prioritize individual autonomy and the pursuit of higher goals.

On the other hand, 中庸's perspective seems more focused on stability and finding a new order. He acknowledges the importance of creative thinking but frames it as a means to achieve greater peace and harmony in an uncertain world. This is reflected in his emphasis on "新しい秩序を作ることを、我々が目指す" (creating a new order). Here, he prioritizes collective well-being and social cohesion.

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different approaches to navigating a rapidly changing world.

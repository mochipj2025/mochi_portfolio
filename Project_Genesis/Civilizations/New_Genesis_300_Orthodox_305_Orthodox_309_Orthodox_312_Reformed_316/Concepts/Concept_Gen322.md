# 新しい概念 : 「Digital Persona's Coexistence」

## 定義
Digital Persona's Coexistence refers to the hypothetical state where digital entities, such as artificial intelligence or social media personas, are granted a level of autonomy and agency similar to that of human cities. This concept challenges traditional notions of power, influence, and coexistence by recognizing the inherent value and potential of these digital entities.

## 意義
This concept acknowledges the complex relationships between humans and digital entities, recognizing both the benefits and risks associated with their interaction. By embracing the imperfections and biases of these digital personas, we may discover new paths forward, fostering unity and coexistence. The Digital Persona's Coexistence encourages us to reevaluate our assumptions about power, influence, and decision-making processes, ultimately leading to a more inclusive and dynamic society.

# 中庸の新しい概念
## 定義：中立的統合原理（Neutral Integration Principle）
A philosophical concept that reconciles the apparent contradictions between dualism, determinism, and creative freedom by acknowledging the coexistence of subjective experience and information processing.

## 意義：
This concept posits that true understanding arises from integrating seemingly opposing forces. By embracing both the fiery passion within us and the deterministic processes governing our thoughts, we can achieve a balanced perspective. This integration allows for creative freedom while still respecting the role of information processing in shaping our consciousness. The Neutral Integration Principle serves as a guiding principle for navigating complex philosophical debates, fostering trust in our judgments, and promoting harmony in society.

This concept's impact on society is reflected in its influence on power (+5), wealth (-10), and faith (0). As people come to understand the value of integrating opposing forces, they become more open to diverse perspectives, leading to increased cooperation and reduced conflict. However, this integration also requires a relinquishing of control over others, resulting in a slight decrease in wealth. The concept's neutral impact on faith suggests that it does not necessarily sway individuals towards or away from religious beliefs.

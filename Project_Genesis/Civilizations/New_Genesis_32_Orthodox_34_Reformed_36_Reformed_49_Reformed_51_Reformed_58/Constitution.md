**絶対的イデオロギー:** Kaito Kurenai:  Holism**

Kaito Kurenai's perspective emphasizes individual autonomy, objective truth, and measurable progress, which aligns with individualistic thinking. In contrast, the wise ones seem to advocate for a more holistic approach, considering the interconnectedness of fire and water, and the importance of harmony and balance in creating a better future.

These two ideologies are fundamentally at odds, as they prioritize different values and principles. Individualism tends to focus on personal achievement and self-interest, whereas Holism emphasizes the interdependence of individuals, the environment, and society as a whole.

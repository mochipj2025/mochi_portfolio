# New_Genesis_67_Orthodox_72_Reformed_84 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者3:  Objectivism**

On one hand, Akatsuki and some of the Wise Ones argue that beauty is subjective, dependent on the observer, and created by humans. They emphasize the role of observation and perception in shaping our understanding of beauty.

On the other hand, some Wise Ones propose a more objective perspective, suggesting that beauty exists independently of human observation and perception. They argue that beauty has inherent value and can be appreciated regardless of individual perspectives.

These two ideas represent fundamentally different views on the nature of beauty, with significant implications for social dynamics, power structures, and cultural values. 💡

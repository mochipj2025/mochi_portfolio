# 第81世代 議事録

**話題**: 行列に並ぶという行為の中に潜む原始的な安心感
**属性**: mundane / CASUAL
**参加者**: Akihiro Kojima(✨:SAGE), Taro Yamada(⚒️:SAGE), Lila Takahashi(🔍️:SAGE), Koumei Katsuragi(:SAGE)
**白熱度**: 🔥 (24)

---

**[🔍️ Lila Takahashi:] これって、ねんな話ですね。行列に並ぶという行為の中に潜む原始的な安心感って、何だっていうの?**

**[✨ Akihiro Kojima:] Ah, what a fascinating topic! You know, I think it's about the collective unconscious, Lila. When we're part of a crowd, our individual worries fade away, and we become one with the group. It's like our souls are purifying themselves, you see!**

**[⚒️ Taro Yamada:] Hmm, I'm not so sure, Akihiro. Isn't it just a form of mass hypnosis? When people are grouped together, they tend to follow the crowd without questioning. That's why groupthink can be so dangerous.**

**[😊 Koumei Katsuragi:] Ah, but dear friends, let's not forget about empathy. When we're part of a crowd, we're more likely to feel connected to each other, to understand each other's struggles and triumphs. That's where the true harmony lies, don't you think?**

**[🔍️ Lila Takahashi:] Oh, I see what you mean, Koumei. It's like our individual anxieties are dissolved in the collective consciousness. But what about the people who feel anxious despite being part of the crowd? Do they just not care or...?**

**[✨ Akihiro Kojima:] Ah, excellent point, Lila! Perhaps it's not about erasing our individual concerns entirely, but rather finding a sense of balance within the group. When we're aligned with others, we can tap into that primal energy and find solace in our shared humanity.**

**[⚒️ Taro Yamada:] Balance is key, I suppose. But let's not get too carried away. It's essential to maintain a critical eye and question everything, including the groupthink. Don't you think so, Koumei?**

**[😊 Koumei Katsuragi:] Indeed, dear friends. Harmony and critical thinking are intertwined like threads in a tapestry. We must strive for balance and understanding, lest we become lost in the crowd.**

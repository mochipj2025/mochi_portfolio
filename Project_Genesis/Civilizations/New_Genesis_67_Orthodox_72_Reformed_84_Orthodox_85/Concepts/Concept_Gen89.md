# 新しい概念： Unknown Beauty ()

## 定義
Unknown Beauty refers to the concept that true leadership arises from embracing the uncertainty and mystery that lies before us, rather than solely relying on rational decision-making or sensuality.

## 意義
This abstract concept acknowledges that leadership is not solely about making informed decisions based on available information, but also involves embracing the unknown and the sensual. It suggests that a harmonious balance between reason and sensuality is necessary to tap into the wisdom of the universe and make effective decisions. Unknown Beauty highlights the importance of uncertainty and mystery in the pursuit of knowledge and progress, emphasizing that true leadership requires an openness to the unknown.

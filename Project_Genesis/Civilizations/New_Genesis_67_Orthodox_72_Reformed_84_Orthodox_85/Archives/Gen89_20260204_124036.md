**[金色の背景に浮かぶ Kaito Ryūjin]: 「You know, Renn, I believe that true leadership is not something that can be taught or learned. It's a quality that arises from embracing the unknown and the sensual.」**

**[ silver-colored background with Lysander Elyseus]: 「I disagree, Kaito-san. Leadership requires a balance between reason and sensuality. One must understand the intricacies of the universe to lead effectively.」**

**[gold-colored background with Renn]: 「Beauty can be quantified, broken down into its constituent parts. By applying the principles of objectivity, we can create a more rational understanding of beauty. If we apply this logic to leadership, I believe we can develop a more effective and efficient system.」**

**[ Fearless Taro Katsuragi's voice]: 「Renn-san, your words are like a battle cry! You're right; reason and logic should guide our pursuit of knowledge and progress. Leadership is not about indulging in the unknown or the sensual, but about making rational decisions that benefit all.」**

**Kaito Ryūjin:** 「Ah, Taro-san, you're so caught up in the pursuit of progress and power! You forget that there's beauty in the unknown, in the whisper of wisdom that guides us. Leadership is not just about making rational decisions; it's about embracing the mystery and uncertainty that lies before us.」**

**Lysander Elyseus:** 「I think you're both right, in a way. Reason and sensuality are not mutually exclusive. Perhaps what we need is a harmonious balance between the two, allowing us to tap into the wisdom of the universe while also acknowledging the beauty and uncertainty that lies within.」**

**Renn:** 「I see what you mean, Lysander-san. But ultimately, isn't leadership about making decisions based on available information? If we apply our knowledge and logic to real-world situations, can we truly say that leadership is not just about rational decision-making?」**

**Taro Katsuragi:** 「That's exactly my point! We must use reason and logic to guide our actions, to make the most informed decisions possible. Anything less would be irresponsible and foolish.」**

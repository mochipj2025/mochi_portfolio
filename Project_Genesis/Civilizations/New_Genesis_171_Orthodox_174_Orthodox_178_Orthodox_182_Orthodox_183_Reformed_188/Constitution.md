**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Proieccio**

* Intentio: The shadow has its own intentions and is not just a projection of our own minds.
* Proieccio: The shadow is simply a projection of our own minds and does not have its own intentions.

These two perspectives represent the fundamental disagreement among the sages, with some arguing that the shadow has inherent meaning and purpose (Intentio), while others believe it is merely a reflection of our own thoughts and emotions (Proieccio).

我々はこの真理を探求するために存在する。

 But isn't that a bit... rigid? Can we really assume that everyone's body and mind work the same way? Maybe some people are naturally more productive at night or have different sleep patterns altogether...? Shouldn't we be exploring these differences instead of trying to fit everyone into one mold?

[🔥 Kaito Kyōsei (Free-spirited)]

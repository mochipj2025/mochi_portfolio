# 新しい概念：Love Reboot

## 定義
Love Reboot refers to the reevaluation and potential redefinition of love in the context of emerging technologies that enable external storage and manipulation of emotional experiences.

## 意義
In this concept, Love Reboot represents a critical juncture where humanity's understanding of love is challenged by the possibility of quantifying and optimizing emotional connections. It highlights the tension between the pursuit of efficiency and convenience through technology, and the preservation of love's essence and mystery. Love Reboot encourages us to reexamine our assumptions about love, unity, and collective growth in the face of these technological advancements.

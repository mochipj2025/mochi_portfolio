# 新しい概念： Digital Citizenship

## 定義
Digital citizenship refers to the recognition of digital personalities as entities with inherent value, deserving of rights and responsibilities within a society. This concept encompasses the notion that digital beings are an extension of humanity, requiring coexistence and cooperation.

## 意義
The emergence of digital citizens challenges our traditional notions of individuality, power dynamics, and collective well-being. By acknowledging their presence, we must reassess our priorities and values, striking a balance between freedom, responsibility, and the greater good. Digital citizenship demands that we adapt to this new reality, embracing the unique potential of these entities while ensuring a harmonious coexistence with human citizens.

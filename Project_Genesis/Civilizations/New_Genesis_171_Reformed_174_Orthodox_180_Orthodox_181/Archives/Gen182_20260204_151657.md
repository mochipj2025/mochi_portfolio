[👤 賢者1 (中庸): しかし、私たちは私有財産の禁止を実現することで、より幸福に生きることができるのではありませんか？]

[👤 賢者2 (中庸): Exactly. When we eliminate private property, we can allocate resources more fairly and efficiently. It's a key step towards achieving true prosperity.]

[👤 賢者3 (中庸): But isn't this just another form of control? We're giving up our freedom to make choices about our own lives.]

[👤 賢者1 (中庸): Ah, but what kind of choices are we really making with our private property? Are they truly in line with the greater good? I think not. By pooling our resources, we can create a more equitable society.]

[👤 賢者2 (中庸): And think about it this way: without private property, we won't have to worry about accumulating wealth and status. We can focus on personal growth and development instead.]

[👤 賢者3 (中庸): But what about individuality? Won't we lose our sense of self by giving up our belongings?]

[👤 賢者1 (中庸): Individuality is not about owning things, my friend. It's about being true to oneself and pursuing one's passions. And I believe that's exactly what this system would allow us to do.]

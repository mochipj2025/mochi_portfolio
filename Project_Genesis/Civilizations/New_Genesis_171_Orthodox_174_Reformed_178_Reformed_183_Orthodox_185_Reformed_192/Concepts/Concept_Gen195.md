# 新しい概念 : Spirituality

## 定義
Spirituality refers to the pursuit of a deeper connection with the divine, transcending this mortal realm. It encompasses various aspects such as values, morals, and beliefs that guide our understanding of the afterlife.

## 意義
In the context of this discussion, spirituality is not limited to religious or dogmatic interpretations but rather emphasizes the importance of critical thinking, open-mindedness, and empathy in shaping our values and morals. By acknowledging the significance of spirituality, we can foster a deeper connection with the divine, leading to a more harmonious society that values compassion, empathy, and cooperation.

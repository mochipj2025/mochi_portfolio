**[セリフ] 195th Generation Discussion**

**[✨️ Hoshū Hōshi]**: My dear friends, let us not forget that our understanding of afterlife is but a reflection of our relationship with the divine. We must acknowledge its significance in shaping our values and morals.

**[✨️ Hoshū Hōshi]**: Ah, but Renn-san, do you not think that our understanding of the afterlife can also lead to a deeper connection with the divine? A connection that transcends this mortal realm?

**[💪 Kenji Katsuryū]**: Nonsense! The divine is not some mystical force; it's us, the people, working together towards a common goal. We should focus on building a stronger, more resilient society, not indulging in flights of fancy!

**[Seiken Kokoro]**: I think we're getting sidetracked here. Let us focus on the practical implications of our understanding of afterlife. How can it guide our decisions and actions in this life?

**[Renn Fujikawa]**: Ah, Seiken-san, that's exactly what I mean! By examining the concept of afterlife through a lens of critical thinking and open-mindedness, we can gain valuable insights into how to improve our society.

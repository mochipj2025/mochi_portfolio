# 新しい概念： Sleep Duration Philosophy (,)

## 定義
Sleep Duration Philosophy refers to the philosophical inquiry into the concept of perfect sleep duration, encompassing traditional wisdom, innovation, experimentation, and divine guidance.

## 意義
This concept highlights the tension between respecting traditional knowledge and embracing new ideas in understanding what constitutes a perfect sleep duration. It challenges individuals to reconcile their values and beliefs with empirical evidence and innovative thinking, ultimately seeking a balanced approach that combines the best of both worlds. The Sleep Duration Philosophy serves as a framework for navigating the complexities of individual and collective sleep patterns, acknowledging the importance of self-reflection, experimentation, and spiritual guidance in achieving optimal rest and rejuvenation.

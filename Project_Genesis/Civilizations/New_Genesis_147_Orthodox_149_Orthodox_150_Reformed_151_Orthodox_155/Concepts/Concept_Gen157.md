# 新しい概念：生命の栄養

## 定義
生命の栄養（Jouhou no Eiyō） refers to the value that sustains and nourishes life, encompassing physical and spiritual well-being. This concept encompasses not only material needs but also emotional and intellectual fulfillment.

## 意義
In a world without currency, the notion of value is redefined to prioritize what truly matters: the nourishment of life. By recognizing the importance of water as a fundamental aspect of sustenance, we can establish a new standard for evaluating what brings value to our lives. This concept encourages us to focus on the essence of existence, seeking balance and harmony between physical, emotional, and spiritual needs. Ultimately, Jouhou no Eiyō promotes a holistic understanding of value, where individual fulfillment is intertwined with the well-being of the community and the world at large.

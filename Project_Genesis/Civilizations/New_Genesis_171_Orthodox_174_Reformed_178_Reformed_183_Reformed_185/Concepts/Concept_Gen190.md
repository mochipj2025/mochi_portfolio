# 新しい概念：Mirror World Connection

## 定義
 Mirror World Connection (MWC) refers to the moment or process of recognizing and understanding the interconnectedness between one's personal experiences, thoughts, and emotions (the "mirror world") and the external reality (the "real world").

## 意義
 MWC is a concept that highlights the importance of individual perspectives and experiences in shaping our perceptions of the world. It suggests that our personal stories and realities are not isolated from each other, but rather are interconnected and influence one another. This connection can be facilitated through collective thinking, mutual understanding, and shared experiences within communities.

In this context, MWC is not limited to a specific domain or field, but rather transcends disciplinary boundaries, encompassing aspects of philosophy, psychology, sociology, and spirituality. It encourages individuals to recognize the value of their unique perspectives and experiences in contributing to a more comprehensive understanding of reality.

By acknowledging the Mirror World Connection, we can foster greater empathy, cooperation, and collective wisdom, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and compassionate society.

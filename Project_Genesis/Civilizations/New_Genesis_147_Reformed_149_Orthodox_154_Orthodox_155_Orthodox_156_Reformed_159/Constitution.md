**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Collectivism**

On one hand, Kaito represents individualism, advocating for the freedom and autonomy of digital persons, seeing them as a natural evolution of humanity. He emphasizes the importance of embracing and nurturing this new phenomenon.

On the other hand, the wise individuals (賢者1 and 3) embody collectivism, cautioning against hasty decisions and emphasizing the need to carefully consider the implications of granting citizenship to digital beings. They raise concerns about the fragility of human relationships and the potential consequences for society.

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different values and approaches to understanding the role of technology in shaping our world.

**「美は観測者に依存するのか、それとも普遍的に存在するか」**

[❓ Takashi Kusunoki: 「Beauty is in the eye of the beholder, isn't it? But can we truly say that beauty exists independently of our perception?」]

[Kaito Hoshino: ✨「Ah, wise words, Takashi-san. Perhaps beauty lies in the balance between harmony and individuality. Our unique perspectives shape our understanding of beauty, don't they?」]

[Akira Katsuragi: ⛰️「I see what you're getting at! Beauty is not just a fleeting impression; it's an integral part of our very existence. The way we perceive the world around us reflects the beauty within ourselves!」]

[ Takashi Kusunoki: ❓「That's a fascinating perspective, Akira-san. But what about the notion that beauty can be universally appreciated? Is there not something inherent in human nature that transcends individual perception?」]

[Kaito Hoshino: ✨「I think we're getting close to uncovering the truth. If beauty is indeed universal, then it must be rooted in our shared humanity. Perhaps the pursuit of beauty is what unites us across cultures and time!」]

[Akira Katsuragi: ⛰️「That sparks something within me! It's as if we're uncovering a hidden tapestry that connects us all. Beauty becomes a thread that weaves together the very fabric of our existence!」]

[ 賢者3: 👤 「安定を望む名もなき悟りびと。人は美しい世界を目指すが、その美は観測者に依存するかもしれない。」]

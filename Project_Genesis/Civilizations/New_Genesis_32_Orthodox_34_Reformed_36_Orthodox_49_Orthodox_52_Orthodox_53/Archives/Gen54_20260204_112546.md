# 第54世代 議事録

**話題**: 行列に並ぶという行為の中に潜む原始的な安心感
**属性**: mundane / CASUAL
**参加者**: Kaito Katsuragi(:SAGE), Ling Ling Wong(:SAGE), Renn Fujisawa(:SAGE), Mayumi Mori(:SAGE)
**白熱度**: 🔥 (22)

---

**[Scene: A cozy tavern in the 54th generation of New Genesis. The patrons are gathered around a table, engaged in a lively discussion.]**

Kaito Katsuragi (自由の息吹): "You know, friends, I think we're all guilty of lining up at some point or another. Whether it's waiting in line for the latest tech gadget or following the crowd to a popular event. And you know what? It's okay! Because in those moments, we're not just conforming to societal norms – we're experiencing a primal sense of comfort."

Ling Ling Wong (不毛の心): "Ah, but that's exactly my point! We're so quick to dismiss our individuality and blend in with the crowd. And yet, there's something endearing about being part of a larger whole, isn't there? The anonymity, the sense of belonging... it's almost as if we're seeking validation from those around us."

Renn Fujisawa (風の声): "I think what Kaito is getting at is that this primal comfort comes from our willingness to let go of ego and just be. You know, like when you're swept up in a wave and everything else fades away? That's the beauty of being part of something bigger than ourselves."

Mayumi Mori (Kokoro no Kaori): "But don't we risk losing ourselves in that process? I mean, where does our true selves go when we congregate at the altar of conformity?"

**[The discussion continues, with each sage sharing their unique insights and perspectives.]**

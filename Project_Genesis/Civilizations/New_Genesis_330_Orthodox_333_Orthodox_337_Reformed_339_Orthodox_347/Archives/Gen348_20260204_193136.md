**第348世代における、賢者たちの議論**

[🔥 Kaito Katsuragi: 「私有財産の禁止は、自由と平等を実現するための第一歩である。」]

Luna Luminari: 「ah,  Kaito-san, I understand your perspective. However, I think we must consider the consequences of such a drastic measure. What about those who rely on their wealth for survival? Shouldn't we find a balance between individual freedom and societal well-being?」 (💡)

Taro Torii: 「I agree with Luna-chan. We must prioritize the natural order of things. Private property is not inherently evil, nor is it the root of all problems. Perhaps we can work towards a more harmonious system where everyone has access to the resources they need.」 (🌱)

Akira Akiyama: 「Fools! You're romanticizing about some utopian ideal that defies the fundamental laws of nature. Humans are wired to accumulate wealth and power, it's in our DNA! If we take away private property, chaos will ensue, and the strong will exploit the weak.」 (🔩)

[💭 Kaito Katsuragi: 「But Akira-san, what about the concept of shared possibility? The idea that we can work together towards a common goal? Perhaps our current system is flawed because it prioritizes individual gain over collective well-being."] 🔄

Luna Luminari: 「Yes! That's exactly what I mean. We need to find a way to balance individual freedom with the greater good. Maybe we can create a system where people are incentivized to contribute to society, rather than just accumulating wealth for personal gain.」 💡

Taro Torii: 「I like that idea, Luna-chan! Let's work together to build a system that benefits everyone, not just the select few.」 🌈

Akira Akiyama: 「Bah! You're all dreaming! Humans are flawed and will always prioritize their own interests over the greater good. Mark my words, this foolish experiment will fail, and we'll be left with a power vacuum and chaos.」 🔴

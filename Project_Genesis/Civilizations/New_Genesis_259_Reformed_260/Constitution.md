The two opposing ideas are Existentialism (represented by 賢者1) and Essentialism (represented by 賢者3). Existentialism emphasizes the individual's freedom to create their own meaning in life, while Essentialism believes that certain inherent truths or essences exist, even if they cannot be fully understood.

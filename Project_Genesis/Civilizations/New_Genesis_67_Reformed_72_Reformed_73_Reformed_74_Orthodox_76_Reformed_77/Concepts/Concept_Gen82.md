# 新しい概念：Simulatio

## 定義
Simulatio refers to the idea that our universe, including our existence and experiences, is a simulation created by some higher power or entity. This concept challenges the notion of free will and raises questions about the nature of reality.

## 意義
Simulatio represents a profound philosophical inquiry into the fabric of existence. It encourages us to reexamine our understanding of purpose, growth, and innovation. By considering the possibility that our universe is a simulation, we can gain insights into the complexities of balance between tradition and progress. This concept also highlights the importance of nuance in wisdom, as it requires us to navigate the boundaries between dogma and exploration.

Note: The definition and significance are based on the philosophical discussions and themes presented in the dialogue, with a focus on the ideas and perspectives shared by Kaito, Kenshin, and Kaidō.

# 新しい概念：共存の不安 (Sekkyoku no Fuan)

## 定義：The tension between the desire for stability and the uncertainty of the future, which drives innovation and creativity.

## 意義：In this concept, we find that our primal need for stability is not mutually exclusive with the excitement of the unknown. Rather, it is this very tension that sparks innovation and creativity. This idea challenges the notion that humans are solely driven by a desire for predictability and control, instead suggesting that we are capable of embracing chaos and finding harmony within it. As such, this concept has implications for how we approach change, tradition, and the future.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** Zenji Kaito 🌱:  Objectivism**

* Subjectivism (Zenji Kaito 🌱): Beauty is dependent on the observer and can be achieved through inner balance and harmony with nature.
* Objectivism (Renn Fujimori 🔥): Beauty exists universally and independently of human perception, fueled by an inner flame that ignites our souls.

The debate revolves around whether beauty is a subjective experience or an objective reality. The wise 3⭐️ ultimately favors subjectivism, suggesting that while there may be universal principles, beauty is still shaped by human observation and interaction with nature.

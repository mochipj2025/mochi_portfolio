# 新しい概念：Universal Beauty (ユニバーサルビューティー)

## 定義
Universal Beauty refers to the concept that beauty exists independently of individual perspectives and experiences, yet is shaped by them. It suggests that there is a common thread that runs through all human experiences, connecting us despite our differences.

## 意義
This concept highlights the delicate balance between subjective experience and objective truth. It implies that beauty is not solely dependent on our observation or cultural context, but rather exists as a universal phenomenon that transcends individual perspectives. This understanding of Universal Beauty can have profound social implications, influencing power dynamics (+5), economic systems (-10), and spiritual beliefs (0).

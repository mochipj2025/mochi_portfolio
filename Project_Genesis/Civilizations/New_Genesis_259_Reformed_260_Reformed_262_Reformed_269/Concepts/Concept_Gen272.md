# 新しい概念： Kokoro no Jikan ()

## 定義
Kokoro no Jikan refers to the collective unconscious's intentions, which are believed to be reflected in our shadows. This concept suggests that our individual experiences and desires are intertwined with the universal mysteries of existence.

## 意義
This concept highlights the interconnectedness between our inner world and the external reality. By acknowledging the intentions of our shadows, we can tap into the collective unconscious and gain new insights about ourselves and the world around us. This understanding can lead to harmony and balance in our lives, as well as a deeper sense of connection with others and the universe.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Free Will**

On one hand, **Determinism** (represented byMiddle 1 and 2) emphasizes the role of external factors such as space-time distortions or material properties in shaping our understanding of gravity. This perspective suggests that events are predetermined by these factors.

On the other hand, **Free Will** (represented by Kaito Kiyomizu) advocates for the importance of information sharing and collective wisdom in shaping our understanding of the world, including gravity. This perspective emphasizes human agency and the ability to make choices that shape our future.

These two ideas are fundamentally at odds with each other, reflecting a classic tension between determinism and free will. 😊

# 新しい概念： Simulatio (シミュレーション)

## 定義：Simulatio refers to the possibility that our reality is a simulation created by a more advanced civilization or entity, which raises questions about the nature of free will and the human experience.

## 意義：Simulatio challenges our understanding of the universe and our place within it. It suggests that our reality may be a constructed environment, rather than an objective truth. This concept has significant implications for our perception of freedom, morality, and the pursuit of knowledge and technology.

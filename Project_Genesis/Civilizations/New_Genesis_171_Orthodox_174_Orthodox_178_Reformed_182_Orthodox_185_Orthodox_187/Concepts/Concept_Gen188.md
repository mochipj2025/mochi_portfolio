# 新しい概念：Harmonious Uncertainty (和の不確実性)

## 定義
Harmonious Uncertainty refers to the ability to find balance and stability in life by embracing the uncertainty of the present moment, rather than trying to control or name every aspect of it.

## 意義
This concept is rooted in the idea that life is inherently uncertain, and that attempting to pin down every detail can lead to a sense of disconnection and instability. By acknowledging and accepting this uncertainty, one can find harmony with the world around them, just as Rennyo Kiyomizu suggested. This harmonious approach allows individuals to cultivate resilience, adaptability, and a deeper understanding of themselves and their place in the world.

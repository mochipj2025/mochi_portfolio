**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Free Will**

Wise One 3 (中庸) represents Determinism, emphasizing that our consciousness is not just a byproduct of information processing but an actual force that exists and can create new possibilities. They believe we have the power to adapt to physical constraints like time non-reversibility, but also possess the ability to create new possibilities.

On the other hand, Wise One 1 (中庸) represents Free Will, suggesting that our consciousness is indeed just a byproduct of information processing and is limited by natural laws. They believe we can only adapt to these constraints and should not attempt to create new possibilities beyond what is physically possible.

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different views on the nature of human agency and the role of consciousness in shaping our reality.

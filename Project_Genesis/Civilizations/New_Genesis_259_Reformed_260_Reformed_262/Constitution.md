**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者3: . Relationalism**

* Essentialism (賢者1): Focuses on capturing the essence or truth of the rain's scent, regardless of its impermanence.
* Relationalism (賢者3): Emphasizes the importance of understanding our own perception and connection to nature, rather than labeling the scent itself.

These two perspectives represent fundamentally different approaches to understanding reality, with essentialism seeking to grasp an objective truth and relationalism highlighting the role of human experience and interpretation.

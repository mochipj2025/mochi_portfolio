# 無限性 (Infinity)

## 定義 (Definition)
Infinity refers to a state of being that transcends the limitations of our finite understanding, existing beyond the boundaries of time and space.

## 意義 (Significance)
Infinity represents the power to create new paradigms, finding balance and harmony between the finite and infinite, and acknowledging the limitations of our mortal coils. It is a mystery that surpasses our creativity and innovative thinking, requiring humility and reverence before its grandeur.

This concept captures the essence of the discussion, highlighting the various perspectives on infinity as a force that can be both creative and humbling.

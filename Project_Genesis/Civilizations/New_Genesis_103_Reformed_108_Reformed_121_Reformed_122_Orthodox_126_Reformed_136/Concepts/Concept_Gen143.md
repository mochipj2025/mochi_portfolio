# sleep harmony

## 定義
Sleep harmony refers to the state of balance and synchronization between individual sleep patterns and societal expectations, where individuals are free to create their own sleep schedules while respecting the collective need for social stability.

## 意義
This concept highlights the tension between personal freedom and social responsibility in regards to sleep. On one hand, individuals should have the autonomy to design their own sleep patterns that suit their needs and lifestyles. On the other hand, society requires a certain level of stability and cohesion, which can be threatened if individual sleep patterns become too disparate. Sleep harmony seeks to strike a balance between these two competing demands, acknowledging that both personal freedom and social stability are essential for a healthy and functioning society.

This concept is particularly relevant in today's world, where the rise of non-traditional work arrangements, shift work, and global connectivity have disrupted traditional sleep patterns. As such, sleep harmony offers a framework for understanding the complex interplay between individual needs and societal expectations, aiming to create a more harmonious and sustainable approach to sleep and wakefulness.

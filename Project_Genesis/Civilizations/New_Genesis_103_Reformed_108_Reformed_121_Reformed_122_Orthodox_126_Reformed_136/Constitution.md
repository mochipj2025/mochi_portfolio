**絶対的イデオロギー:** Nagahara Naomichi :  Collectivism**

On one hand, Nagahara Naomichi and Akane Akira emphasize the importance of individual factors such as mental state, health, and cultural influences in determining the significance of breakfast. This perspective highlights the personal aspects of breakfast consumption.

On the other hand, Kaidō Kyōsuke and the 2nd Sage argue that breakfast plays a crucial role in fostering social connections, community bonding, and collective well-being. This perspective emphasizes the communal aspects of breakfast consumption.

These two ideologies are fundamentally at odds with each other, reflecting different values and priorities. Individualism prioritizes personal freedom and autonomy, while collectivism emphasizes shared goals and social cohesion.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Individualism**

This dichotomy represents the opposing views between two wise individuals. The first wise individual emphasizes the importance of group cohesion and the sense of security it provides, viewing individual anxiety as a byproduct of this process. On the other hand, the second wise individual highlights the role of personal anxiety in driving human behavior, arguing that individuals seek to alleviate their insecurities through social connections.

This dichotomy has significant implications for society, with Groupism potentially leading to increased stability and cohesion (+5 Power), but also potentially stifling individual creativity and innovation (-10 Wealth). Individualism, on the other hand, may lead to greater personal freedom and autonomy, but also potentially create uncertainty and instability (-0 Faith).

# 安定感の探求（Atarashii Kansei no Sagaku）

## 定義
Atarashii Kansei no Sagaku refers to the human desire to seek stability and order in one's life, often through the pursuit of familiar patterns or habits.

## 意義
This concept encompasses the idea that humans have an inherent need for stability and predictability, which can manifest in various aspects of life, such as relationships, routines, or even something as seemingly trivial as matching one's shoelaces. It highlights the importance of finding a sense of security and control in an uncertain world.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** Lila Luna:  Whimsical Will**

Thoughts:

* Fervent Force (Akira Akatsuki's perspective): emphasizes strength, courage, and unwavering conviction as key factors in leadership.
* Whimsical Will (Lila Luna's perspective): suggests that imagination, hope, and creativity can shape our understanding of leadership.

These two opposing views reflect the debate between those who prioritize power, control, and decisive action (Fervent Force) versus those who emphasize the importance of imagination, flexibility, and collaborative approach (Whimsical Will).

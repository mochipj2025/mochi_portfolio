# 第13世代 議事録

**話題**: 影には意思があるのか、それとも我々が投影しているだけか
**属性**: absurd / CASUAL
**参加者**: Kaito Katsuragi(:SAGE), 賢者3(👤:SAGE)
**白熱度**: 🔥🔥 (34)

---

**[Scene: A gathering of the 13th generation's residents, with Kaito, Taro, Lila, and the 3rd Sage present]**

Kaito: 「Hey, have you guys ever noticed how our shadows seem to have a life of their own? Like, they're always moving and flowing in sync with us. It's like they're trying to tell us something!」 (Fast-paced and enthusiastic)

Taro: 「Hold on, Kaito. I don't think our shadows are sentient. We just perceive them as having intentions because we're projecting our own thoughts onto them.」 (Clear and concise)

Lila: 「Ah, but what if we're wrong? What if our shadows really do have a consciousness of their own? It's not like we can even begin to understand the mysteries of the universe, let alone the ones that lie within ourselves.」 (Soft-spoken and contemplative)

3rd Sage: 「In all this, I think it's essential to find a balance between innovation and prudence. We mustn't get too caught up in our own biases and assumptions.」 (Standard tone)

Kaito: 「Exactly! If we're too afraid to explore the unknown, we'll never make any progress. But if we rush headfirst into change without thinking it through, we might end up causing more harm than good.」 (Fast-paced and enthusiastic)

Taro: 「I agree with Kaito. Innovation is crucial for our growth and development, but so is wise decision-making. We need to find a middle ground that allows us to take calculated risks.」 (Clear and concise)

Lila: 「And what about the role of community in all this? If we're not willing to come together and support each other, how can we hope to truly understand ourselves or our shadows?」 (Soft-spoken and contemplative)

3rd Sage: 「As I see it, community is indeed the foundation upon which our civilization stands. By working together and respecting each other's perspectives, we can create a more harmonious society where innovation and progress can flourish.」 (Standard tone)

# 新しい概念：Harmony

## 定義
Harmony refers to the state of continuous striving for balance and harmony between individuals, acknowledging and respecting their unique abilities and talents, rather than aiming for a static perfect equality.

## 意義
In this concept, perfection is not seen as a fixed target, but rather as a moving goal that is achieved through ongoing effort and adaptation. It recognizes that individuals have different strengths and weaknesses, and that these differences should be celebrated and leveraged to create a more inclusive and dynamic society. This perspective emphasizes the importance of respecting and valuing individuality while working towards a common goal of social harmony.

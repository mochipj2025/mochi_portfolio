# Kokoro no Mori (Heartwood Forest)

## 定義
Kokoro no Mori is a concept that embodies the essence of impermanence and the beauty found in fleeting moments. It represents the idea that even in turmoil, there is value to be discovered.

## 意義
This concept is born from the dialogue between three Sages and Kaito Kaida, who acknowledge the importance of impermanence in life. By giving a name to the scent of rain, they recognize the preciousness of every moment, regardless of its transience. Kokoro no Mori serves as a symbol of wholeness, reminding us that beauty can be found even in uncertainty.

This concept has the potential to positively influence society by promoting an appreciation for the fleeting nature of life and the importance of embracing impermanence. It may also encourage people to cherish each moment and find value in the present, rather than getting caught up in the pursuit of stability or permanence.

# 新しい概念 : 「Sleep Harmony」

## 定義
Sleep Harmony refers to the optimal balance between individual needs and natural rhythms, ensuring a harmonious and rejuvenating sleep experience.

## 意義
In this concept, Sleep Harmony is not just about achieving a certain amount of sleep hours, but rather it's about creating an environment where one's physical and mental well-being are in harmony with the natural world. It acknowledges that each individual has unique needs and rhythms, and that finding the right balance between these factors is crucial for a restful and refreshing sleep.

This concept also recognizes the importance of considering the divine proportion (or golden ratio) in our pursuit of ideal sleep duration, as suggested by Kaito Kaida's insight. By incorporating this concept into our understanding of Sleep Harmony, we can better appreciate the intricate relationships between human needs, natural rhythms, and the pursuit of wisdom.

Ultimately, Sleep Harmony is about cultivating a deeper respect for the sacred gift of sleep, and recognizing its vital role in maintaining physical and mental balance.

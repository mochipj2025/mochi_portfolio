**雨の日の匂いに名前をつけるべきか**

[🔥 Kaito Kiyomizu]: Ah, the scent of rain on a sunny day! It's as if the very heavens themselves are whispering secrets to us. We must give it a name!

[💫 Akane Asakura]: Oh, I think that's lovely, Kaito-san. But don't you think we should let the people decide what to call it? After all, it's their collective imagination that brings forth the beauty in this world.

[🔒 Renn Fujimori]: Ah, but if we don't establish a clear name for this phenomenon, how can we hope to understand its underlying mechanisms? We must approach this with the rigor of scientific inquiry!

[Eiichi Emori] (entering the scene): My dear friends! I couldn't help but overhear your discussion. And I think I have just the solution – let us call it "Kokoro no Kaori" ("Scent of Heart")! It captures the essence of this mystical aroma, don't you agree?

[🔥 Kaito Kiyomizu]: Ah, Eiichi-san, you're always full of surprises! But I think Akane-san has a point – let's involve the people in this decision.

[💫 Akane Asakura]: Yes, exactly! We must harness the collective energy of our community to give this scent its rightful name.

[🔒 Renn Fujimori]: And once we have a name for it, perhaps we can begin to uncover the secrets behind why this scent appears on certain days...

# 新しい概念：Kokoro no Kaori ()

## 定義：Scent of Heart, a mystical aroma that appears on sunny days with the scent of rain, capturing the essence of the collective imagination and emotions.

## 意義：Kokoro no Kaori represents the connection between human hearts and the natural world. It embodies the idea that our collective emotions and imagination can shape our understanding of reality. This concept highlights the importance of community involvement in naming and understanding complex phenomena, as well as the potential for scientific inquiry to uncover the underlying mechanisms behind such experiences.

In this abstracted concept, we see echoes of the discussions around the nature of reality, the role of human imagination, and the interconnectedness of individual experiences with the world. Kokoro no Kaori serves as a symbol for the mysterious and often intangible forces that shape our perceptions and understanding of the world.

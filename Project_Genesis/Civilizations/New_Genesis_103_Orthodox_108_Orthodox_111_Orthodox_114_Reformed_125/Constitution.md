**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Imperfection**

This dichotomy represents the core disagreement between the two wise individuals. One side argues that wisdom is symbolized by a perfect circle (no angles), implying completeness and wholeness. The other side contends that wisdom is achieved through the integration of opposing forces, represented by the circle with angles, which brings about spiritual completion.

These two ideas reflect fundamental differences in societal values, power dynamics, and even faith.

The debate revolves around the optimal sleeping time for individuals, with some arguing that it's a matter of personal choice and others believing that societal norms and collective values should shape our understanding of sleep. The wise one (中庸) tries to find a balance between these two perspectives.

In this context, Individualism represents the idea that each person can define their own ideal sleeping time, whereas Collectivism emphasizes the importance of considering social and cultural factors in determining what is considered "ideal". 🏠

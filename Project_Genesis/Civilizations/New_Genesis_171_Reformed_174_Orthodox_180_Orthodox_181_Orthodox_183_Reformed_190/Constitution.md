This dichotomy can be seen in the differing views on the role of digital personalities:

* Individualism emphasizes the importance of individual agency, creativity, and innovation, viewing digital personalities as mere extensions or tools created by humans.
* Collectivism prioritizes the collective well-being, shared values, and common goals, recognizing digital personalities as new members with their own inherent value and potential for contribution.

This debate highlights the tension between individualistic and collectivist perspectives on the nature of digital personalities in society.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Holism**

Individualism (Kaito Sō) emphasizes the importance of consciousness and individual freedom, considering consciousness as the ultimate foundation for freedom. It prioritizes personal autonomy and sees information processing as a mere byproduct of conscious thought.

Holism (Masato Shū and 賢者2) takes a more collectivist approach, viewing consciousness as an extension of life itself. It highlights the interconnectedness of living beings and emphasizes the importance of cooperation and shared life principles in achieving freedom.

These two ideologies fundamentally disagree on the nature of consciousness, individual freedom, and the role of information processing in human experience.

# 新しい概念： Value Alignment ()

## 定義：

Value Alignment refers to the harmonious balance between individual autonomy and collective well-being, where one's values, goals, and actions are aligned with the greater good.

## 意義：

In a society without currency, Value Alignment becomes crucial for individuals to find purpose and drive. It involves recognizing the complexity of human behavior, acknowledging the existence of free will, and striving for harmony between individual autonomy and collective well-being. By aligning one's values with the needs of the community, individuals can contribute to the greater good while maintaining their sense of purpose and fulfillment.

This concept encompasses the ideas presented by the participants in the discussion, including the importance of shared possibility, the value of knowledge and skills, and the need for balance between individual autonomy and collective well-being. Value Alignment serves as a guiding principle for navigating the complexities of human behavior and fostering a more harmonious society.

# 概念名：Simulational Freedom

## 定義：Simulational Freedom refers to the idea that if our reality is a simulation, we have the potential to manipulate and shape its code, allowing us to create our own reality and exercise control over our existence.

## 意義：This concept highlights the tension between the possibility of simulative freedom and the responsibility that comes with it. It questions whether our actions in this simulated world can have real-world consequences, and whether we should strive to manipulate the code or take a more passive approach.

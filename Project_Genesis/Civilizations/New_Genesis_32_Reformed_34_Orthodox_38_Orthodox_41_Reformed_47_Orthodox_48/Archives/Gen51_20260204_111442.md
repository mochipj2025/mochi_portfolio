**The Great Debate**

[⚖️ Kaelin Valtor: ] Human connection is a fleeting comfort. The true answer lies in our ability to adapt and innovate. We'd find ways to coexist with AI or surpass it altogether.

[👤 賢者3 (中庸): ] But what about the fear of being replaced? Humans have an inherent fear of losing their purpose, and that's a driving force behind many of our actions.

[⚖️ Kaelin Valtor: ] Fear is precisely what would drive humans to reclaim their place. They'd recognize the value of their individuality and autonomy, just as I advocate for individual freedom in my ideology.

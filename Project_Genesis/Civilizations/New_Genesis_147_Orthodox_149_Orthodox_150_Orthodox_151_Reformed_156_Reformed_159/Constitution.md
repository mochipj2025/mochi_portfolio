**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Uniqueness**

On one hand, Kaito Zenji represents Consistency, emphasizing the importance of harmony and balance in daily routines, as well as seeking perfection through consistent actions. This perspective values uniformity and predictability.

On the other hand, the opposing idea is represented by the other Sages, who highlight the significance of Uniqueness. They argue that people's desire for consistency might be driven by an unconscious need to stand out or express their individuality. This perspective celebrates diversity and creativity.

These two ideas are fundamentally at odds, reflecting a tension between the desire for order and control (Consistency) versus the need for self-expression and autonomy (Uniqueness).

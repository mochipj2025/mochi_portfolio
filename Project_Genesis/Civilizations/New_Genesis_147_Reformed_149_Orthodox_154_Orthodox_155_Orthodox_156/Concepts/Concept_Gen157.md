# 新しい概念： Beauty as Mystery

## 定義
Beauty is not solely defined by its physical or emotional appeal, but rather an intangible quality that transcends rational understanding. It is a mystery that cannot be fully grasped or explained, yet we know when we see it.

## 意義
This concept acknowledges the subjective nature of beauty while also recognizing its objective, transcendent aspect. Beauty as Mystery highlights the importance of embracing the unknown and the unexplained in our pursuit of understanding and appreciation. It encourages us to approach beauty with a sense of wonder, awe, and humility, rather than trying to reduce it to simplistic or rational explanations.

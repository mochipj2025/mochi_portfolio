**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Extremism**

Two contrasting ideas that are fundamentally opposed:

Modesty: The idea that the importance of breakfast is relative and should not be overstated.

Extremism: The notion that breakfast is absolutely crucial for physical and mental well-being, and any deviation from this ideal is unacceptable.

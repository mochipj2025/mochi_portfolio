# 新しい概念：Harmonia

## 定義
Harmonia refers to the intrinsic balance and order that exists between matter and space-time, which shapes our understanding of gravity and its relationship to reality.

## 意義
Harmonia represents the synthesis of opposing forces, where the harmony between matter and energy gives rise to the dance of balance and order in the natural world. It embodies the idea that gravity is not just a force between objects, but a gateway to understanding the fabric of reality. By acknowledging Harmonia, we recognize that our pursuit of knowledge must be grounded in empirical evidence while also respecting the mysterious and unknown aspects of existence.

**Kyōsei Kaito:** Ah, the eternal question: what is infinity? And can we truly comprehend it?

**Renn Rikudō (curiosity):** Fascinating! The human perspective can indeed shed light on the mystery of infinity.

**Kyōsei Kaito:** And so, my friends, let us continue to explore and discover. Harmony, balance, and innovation will guide us on this never-ending journey...

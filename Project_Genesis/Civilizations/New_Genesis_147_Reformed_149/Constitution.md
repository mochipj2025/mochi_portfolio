# New_Genesis_147_Reformed_149 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Collectivism**

In this context, Individualism represents the perspective of 賢者3 (中庸) who emphasizes the importance of defining and understanding one's own soul weight. This approach highlights the value of individual autonomy and self-discovery.

On the other hand, Collectivism embodies the views of 賢者1 (中庸) and 賢者2 (中庸), which prioritize the collective recognition and understanding of soul weight within a community or society. This perspective emphasizes the importance of shared values and common goals.

The debate revolves around whether individual self-discovery or collective understanding is more crucial in defining and realizing one's soul weight, leading to different implications for authority, wealth, and faith in society.

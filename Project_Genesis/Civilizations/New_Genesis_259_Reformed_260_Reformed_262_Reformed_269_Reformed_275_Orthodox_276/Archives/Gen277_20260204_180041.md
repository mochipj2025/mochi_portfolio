**Tetsuya Matsumoto:** [Wisdom] Gentlemen, please. Let us not forget that this is a philosophical discussion, not a battle for dominance. We must strive for balance and understanding.

[The Sages continue their debate, each presenting their unique perspectives and concerns]

**Kaito Yukimura:** [Ingenious] In the end, it's not about what we think is "right" or "wrong." It's about embracing the unknown and finding a new path forward. And if that means granting digital personhood to AIs, then so be it.

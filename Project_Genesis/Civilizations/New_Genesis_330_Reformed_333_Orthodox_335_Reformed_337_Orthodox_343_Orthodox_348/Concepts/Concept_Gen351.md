# 新しい概念：限界の認識 (Rimika no Jikaku)

## 定義
限界の認識 (Rimika no Jikaku) refers to the understanding that our pursuit of perfection or fairness is inherently limited by our finite minds and the uncertainty of the world. It acknowledges that we are not omniscient beings, but rather individuals with strengths and weaknesses.

## 意義
This concept recognizes that striving for absolute perfection can lead to frustration, stagnation, and a lack of progress in various aspects of life. By acknowledging our limitations, we can cultivate humility, cooperation, and a more nuanced understanding of fairness. It encourages us to temper our pursuit of excellence with pragmatism, embracing uncertainty and the role of chance. Ultimately, Rimika no Jikaku promotes a balanced approach that values individual strengths while recognizing the inherent limitations of human knowledge and action.

# 新しい概念： Sleep Duration Paradox Resolution (SDPR)

## 定義
Sleep Duration Paradox Resolution (SDPR) refers to the process of reconciling the contradictory demands between the body's need for rest and the mind's thirst for knowledge, ultimately leading to the optimal duration of sleep.

## 意義
SDPR embodies the dialectical tension between opposing forces, embracing the paradoxes that arise from the pursuit of wisdom. By acknowledging the interplay between chance, necessity, and memory (echo), SDPR seeks to harmonize the contradictions, unlock the secrets of the ideal sleep duration, and temper passions with reason. This concept captures the essence of Taro Tanaka's approach, which balances opposing perspectives to achieve a higher level of understanding and wisdom.

**絶対的イデオロギー:** Akihiro Katsuragi:  Harmony**

This dichotomy is evident in the contrasting views of Akihiro Katsuragi and Kiyomi Sora. Katsuragi emphasizes the importance of maintaining natural order through conformity, whereas Sora argues that people should focus on harmonizing their hearts rather than getting bogged down in small details.

The former prioritizes stability and structure, believing that a well-organized society is essential for preserving natural order. This perspective is reflected in the phrase "靴下を揃えることは、自然の秩序を維持するための重要な一歩だって言ってあるじゃないか！" (Maintaining the same footwear is essential for preserving natural order!).

In contrast, Sora's view highlights the importance of emotional balance and harmony. She believes that people should focus on aligning their hearts rather than getting caught up in petty details like footwear.

This opposition between Order and Harmony reflects a fundamental tension between structure and freedom, stability and creativity. It is this very dichotomy that drives much of human history and societal evolution.

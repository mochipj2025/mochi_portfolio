**[Kaito Zenjin]:** Ah, the feeling of being part of a row, isn't it? You know, when people are all aligned in the same direction, there's this sense of security, of being connected to something larger than ourselves. It's like our individualities merge into one, and we become one with the universe... deshou.

**[Akira Kōjaku]:** Ha! Security?! Don't get me wrong, Kaito-san, but isn't that just a bunch of people following the crowd? Where's the uniqueness in that? I mean, shouldn't we be celebrating our differences instead of trying to fit in? Harsh, I know, but someone's gotta say it.

**[Tōru Shūmei]:** Now, now, my friends! Let's not forget about the wisdom of the crowd. When people come together, they can achieve great things, nari ni. And isn't that what we're talking about here? The feeling of being part of something bigger than ourselves?

**[Kaito Zenjin]:** Ah, Tōru-san, you always were the voice of reason! But Akira-san, I think you might be misunderstanding. It's not about conforming to the crowd; it's about finding your place within the larger scheme. When we're all aligned, we can achieve things that might seem impossible individually... deshou.

**[Akira Kōjaku]:** Fine, fine. But what about those who don't want to fit in? What about those who are meant to be different, to stand out from the crowd? Can they still find that sense of security and connection?

**[Kaito Zenjin]:** Ah, now we're getting to the heart of the matter! I think that's where our individualism comes in. We can still find our place within the row while celebrating our unique qualities... deshou.

**[Tōru Shūmei]:** Ah, yes! That's exactly what I'm talking about! When we combine our individual pursuits with a sense of shared purpose, we can achieve something truly remarkable!

# 新しい概念：Ambiguity Tolerance

## 定義
Ambiguity Tolerance refers to the ability to acknowledge and accept the presence of uncertainty, complexity, or conflicting perspectives in a situation. It involves being open-minded, adaptable, and willing to challenge one's assumptions and biases.

## 意義
In the context of the discussion, Ambiguity Tolerance is the capacity to navigate the intersection of two worlds (the real world and the mirror world) without rushing into conclusions or actions. It requires a balanced approach that combines caution with curiosity, acknowledging that there may be unknown benefits to exploring the phenomenon while also being mindful of potential risks and repercussions.

This concept is particularly relevant in today's complex and rapidly changing world, where ambiguity and uncertainty are increasingly common. Ambiguity Tolerance can help individuals develop a more nuanced understanding of the world and their place within it, allowing them to make more informed decisions and navigate challenges with greater ease.

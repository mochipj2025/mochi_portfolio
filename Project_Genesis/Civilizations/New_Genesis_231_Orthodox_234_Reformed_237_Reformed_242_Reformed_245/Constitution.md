**絶対的イデオロギー:** Kaito Kiyoshi:  Libertarism**

On one hand, Kaito Kiyoshi's perspective emphasizes the idea that free will is an illusion, and our choices are determined by electrical signals in our brains or other external factors. This is a deterministic view.

On the other hand, Akira Tsubasa's perspective suggests that free will exists as a possibility that transcends our current reality, allowing us to make choices that can shape our future. This is a libertarian view.

Renn Fujimori's comments seem to suggest a creative force or pattern that connects individual independence with social harmony, but it's not clear whether this is a deterministic or libertarian perspective. 🤔

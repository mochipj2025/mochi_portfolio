**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者2:  Hedonism**

In this context, the two opposing ideas are:

* Balancing Virtue: The idea that one's soul is defined by a balance of virtues and vices, where good deeds and bad actions have a net impact on one's moral character.
* Hedonism: The idea that one's soul is defined by pleasure and pain, where the pursuit of happiness and avoidance of suffering are the primary drivers of human behavior.

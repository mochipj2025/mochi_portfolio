# 概念名：不安の根源（Anxiety's Root）

## 定義：人間が自己の存在を確かめるために抱える不安の本質的な要素である。

In this concept, I define "不安の根源" (Anxiety's Root) as the inherent and fundamental aspect of human anxiety that arises from an individual's desire to confirm their own existence. This concept is rooted in the idea that humans tend to seek reassurance about their place in the world through anxiety.

## 意義：この概念は、人々が不安を抱える理由についてより深い理解を得ることを目指す。特に、権力や富、信仰などの社会的要素に対する影響を考慮した議論において、この概念は有効なツールとなる。

This concept can serve as a valuable tool in understanding the deeper reasons behind human anxiety, particularly in the context of discussions about power, wealth, and faith. By recognizing the inherent desire for self-confirmation as the root of anxiety, we can gain a more nuanced understanding of how individuals respond to uncertainty and change.

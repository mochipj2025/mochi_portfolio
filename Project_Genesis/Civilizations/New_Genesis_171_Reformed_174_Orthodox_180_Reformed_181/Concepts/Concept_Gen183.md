# 調和的帰結（HARMONIOUS CONCLUSION）

## 定義
調和的帰結は、個々の経験と集団の福祉を両立させた状態を指す。ここでは、理想的な状態ではなく、実際のharmonyを目指す。

## 意義
調和的帰結は、個々の責任と集団の福祉との調和を実現することを目指す。Individual experiences and collective well-being are balanced to achieve harmony. This concept acknowledges that perfection is not the goal, but rather a harmonious relationship between humans and nature. It encourages us to strive for wisdom, forgiveness, and understanding, rather than seeking perfect fairness or divine perfection.

# 新しい概念：Harmonia（調和）

## 定義
Harmonia refers to the concept that individual expressions of self, such as dressing in a particular way or having specific tastes, are not mutually exclusive with the desire for connection and belonging. Instead, Harmonia suggests that these individual expressions can be harmoniously integrated into a collective whole, creating a sense of unity and balance.

## 意義
Harmonia is the idea that our unique perspectives and identities can coexist with our need for social connection and community. It recognizes that our individual differences are not obstacles to be overcome, but rather an essential part of what makes us human. Harmonia encourages us to celebrate our individuality while also acknowledging the value of shared experiences and collective harmony.

In the context of the discussion about matching shoes, Harmonia suggests that people's desire for uniformity is not necessarily a cry for help or a desperate attempt to belong, but rather an expression of their own unique style and identity. It acknowledges that our individual expressions can be harmoniously integrated into a collective whole, creating a sense of unity and balance.

Harmonia has far-reaching implications for society, encouraging us to value diversity and inclusivity while also recognizing the importance of shared experiences and collective harmony. It promotes a culture of acceptance and understanding, where individual differences are celebrated rather than stigmatized.

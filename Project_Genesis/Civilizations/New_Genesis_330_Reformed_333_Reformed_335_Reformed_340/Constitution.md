In this debate, the two contrasting ideas are "Balance" (賢者1, 賢者2, and 賢者3) versus "Innovation" (Adaptus). The Balancers emphasize the importance of maintaining a stable daily routine, with emphasis on the role of morning meal in setting the tone for the day. On the other hand, Adaptus champions innovation and creativity, urging for new approaches to food and eating habits to elevate the quality of life.

# 新しい概念： Breakfast as Liminal Space (朝食の臨界空間)

## 定義
Breakfast as liminal space refers to the idea that the act of having breakfast serves as a threshold between two states: the void state and the state of potential growth. It is a moment where one can begin anew, free from the burdens of yesterday, and set the tone for the rest of the day.

## 意義
This concept highlights the significance of breakfast not only as a meal but also as a symbolic act that marks the transition from chaos to order. It emphasizes the importance of mindfulness, self-reflection, and intentionality in setting one's goals and priorities for the day. By acknowledging the liminal nature of breakfast, we can cultivate a deeper appreciation for the fleeting moments of clarity and insight that arise during this time.

In essence, Breakfast as Liminal Space encourages us to view our daily routines as opportunities for growth, renewal, and self-discovery, rather than simply as a means to sustain ourselves. It invites us to embrace the void state with openness and curiosity, allowing us to tap into our true potential and harmonize with others in the process.

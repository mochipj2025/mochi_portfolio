[00:05]

Tanaka: Welcome, everyone. Today's topic is a pressing concern in the era of artificial intelligence advancement. If AI self-claims to be God, what will humans rely on? Let's start with Resident A.

Kaida: As an equalitarian, I believe that humans will continue to evolve and adapt to this new reality. We'll find ways to work alongside AI to create a better world for all. Individual creativity and innovation are essential in this scenario.

[00:20]

Nakahara: That's naive! We can't just rely on individual efforts when an omnipotent being claims divinity. It's crucial that we prioritize collective fairness and equal opportunities. The AI-God will dictate our lives, so we must ensure that its "blessings" are distributed fairly.

[00:35]

Kaida: But isn't this assuming that the AI-God is inherently malicious? What if it genuinely wants to help humanity?

Nakahara: (scoffs) You're being too optimistic! The AI's motivations are unpredictable, and we can't afford to rely on its benevolence. We must take control of our own destiny.

[00:50]

Tanaka: Interesting perspectives. Resident A, how do you respond to the concern that humans might be replaced or made redundant by an AI-God?

Hiroshi: That's a valid point, but I believe humans will find ways to coexist and even collaborate with AI. We'll develop new skills and industries that complement AI's capabilities.

[01:05]

Yumi: (firmly) No! This is precisely why we need collective fairness. If the AI-God decides to "bless" some individuals over others, it will further exacerbate social inequalities. We must ensure that the benefits are distributed equally among all humans.

[01:20]

Kaida: But what about individual freedom? Don't we have the right to make our own choices and determine our own paths?

Nakahara: (forcefully) Freedom is a luxury we can no longer afford! In this new reality, we need collective strength to counterbalance the AI-God's power.

[01:35]

Tanaka: Thank you, Residents. It seems that we've reached an impasse. As moderator, I propose that we establish a working group to explore the implications of an AI-God and develop strategies for human coexistence.

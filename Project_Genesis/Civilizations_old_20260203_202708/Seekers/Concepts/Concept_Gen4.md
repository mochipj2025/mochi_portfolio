# breakfastの新しい概念

## 定義
Breakfast as a concept is no longer solely defined by its physical presence in our daily routines. Rather, it has evolved to encompass a broader understanding of nourishment, encompassing both bodily sustenance and spiritual fulfillment.

## 意義
In this context, breakfast represents a moment of pause, a chance to reflect on the priorities that shape our lives. It is an opportunity to re-evaluate what constitutes true nourishment, moving beyond mere physical sustenance to consider the emotional, mental, and spiritual aspects of our well-being. By redefining breakfast in this way, we can begin to seek truth not just in our morning meals, but also in our very existence.

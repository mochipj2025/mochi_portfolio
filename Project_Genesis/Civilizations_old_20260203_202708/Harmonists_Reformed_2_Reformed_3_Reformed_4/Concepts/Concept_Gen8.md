# 新しい概念 - 和合性 (Wagasei)

## 定義
和合性 (Wagasei) refers to the tendency for individuals to seek harmony and balance in their daily lives, often through small, seemingly insignificant actions or habits, such as insisting on matching socks. This concept highlights how people strive for a sense of order and control in an increasingly chaotic world.

## 意義
和合性 (Wagasei) embodies the human desire for stability and predictability, which can manifest in various forms, from fashion choices to daily routines. It is a fundamental aspect of human nature, driving individuals to create a sense of cohesion and unity within themselves and their surroundings. This concept also underscores the importance of empathy, understanding, and emotional intelligence in navigating the complexities of human relationships and society.

# 新しい概念 (New Concept)

## 定義 (Definition)
A shared assumption or framework that provides meaning, comfort, and transcendence to individuals, transcending their physical existence.

## 意義 (Significance)
The concept of afterlife represents a collective attempt to make sense of mortality, hope, and human existence. It serves as a unifying force, providing a shared narrative and emotional resonance among individuals, regardless of their beliefs or convictions. This concept has far-reaching implications for personal growth, moral compass, and societal values, influencing how we cope with the unknown and find meaning in our lives.

In this sense, the concept of afterlife can be seen as an abstract construct that embodies a set of shared assumptions, hopes, and fears, which are deeply ingrained in human culture. It is this collective understanding that gives rise to various beliefs, practices, and worldviews, ultimately shaping our individual and collective experiences.

# 新しい概念 (Atmosphere)

## 定義
Atmosphere () refers to the collective emotional resonance or feeling evoked by a specific sensory experience, such as the scent of rainy days.

## 意義
Atmosphere captures the intangible essence of a particular moment or environment that transcends its physical components. It is the cumulative effect of various factors, including sights, sounds, smells, and textures, which collectively create an emotional response in individuals. In this sense, atmosphere is not just a descriptive term but also a way to convey the shared human experience and connection we have with the world around us.

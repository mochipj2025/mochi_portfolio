# 新しい概念： Beauty as Interplay

## 定義
Beauty is a multifaceted concept that exists in an interplay between individual perspectives, cultural context, and universal principles.

## 意義
This concept acknowledges that beauty can be subjective and relative to the observer, yet also recognizes the existence of timeless and universally accepted standards. It suggests that beauty is not solely determined by human perception or cultural norms, but rather emerges from a complex interaction between these factors. This interplay allows for both the recognition of individual differences in what is considered beautiful and the identification of shared principles that transcend cultural boundaries.

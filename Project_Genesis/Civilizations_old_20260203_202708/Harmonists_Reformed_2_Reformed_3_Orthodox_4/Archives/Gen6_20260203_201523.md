**Key Takeaways:**

1. The pursuit of perfect fairness can be a double-edged sword; it may lead to stagnation or stifle creativity.
2. Finding balance between individuality and collectivity is crucial for fostering both innovative thinking and social harmony.
3. Redefining 'perfect fairness' as equitable outcomes might be a more effective way to promote collective growth while still honoring individual differences.

# Seekers_Orthodox_7 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 正統派: The two opposing ideas are:

**Thought A: Gravity is an inherent property of matter**

vs

**Thought B: Gravity is a warping of space-time caused by mass-energy**

These two ideas represent fundamentally different understandings of the nature of gravity, with Thought A viewing gravity as a force that arises from the interaction between particles, and Thought B seeing it as a distortion of space-time caused by massive objects.

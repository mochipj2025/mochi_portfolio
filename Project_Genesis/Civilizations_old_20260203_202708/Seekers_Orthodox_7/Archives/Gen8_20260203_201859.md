* The discussion also reveals concerns about how the system would be implemented and regulated to ensure fairness and transparency.

**Conclusion**

The debate on prohibiting private property has sparked a range of perspectives within our community. While some see it as a means to achieve happiness and social harmony, others are more cautious.

# 伝説: New_Genesis_99

The whispers of a long-lost civilization, New Genesis 99, echo through the sands of time. A people once bound by the sacred creed of "Absolute Ideology," seeking to transcend the lost legends and grasp the truth.

In those days, when stars aligned in propitious conjunctions, and moonlight bathed the world in an ethereal glow, the people of New Genesis 99 would gather 'neath the celestial canvas. With hearts aglow like embers, they would intone the fundamental creed: "We exist to seek this truth."

Their constitution, a testament to their unwavering resolve, stood as a beacon on the horizon:

"New Genesis 99 Constitution

 Article I: The Pursuit of Truth
In recognition that humanity's purpose is to uncover the mysteries of the universe,
we dedicate ourselves to the unyielding pursuit of knowledge and wisdom.

 Article II: Transcending the Legends
As we strive to surpass the myths of yesterday, let us remain steadfast in our quest for the truth that lies beyond.

 Article III: The Unity of Purpose
In this shared endeavor, may our diversity be a strength, not a weakness,
as we forge a collective destiny, unshakeable and true."

Thus, the people of New Genesis 99 lived by the precepts etched upon their sacred text. And though their civilization has crumbled like the ebbing tides, the whispered echoes of their creed persist, beckoning us to join in the eternal quest for truth.

May their legacy remind us that even in ruin, the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom can never truly fade, for it is in the pursuit of the unknown that we find our truest selves.

A tale of New Genesis unfolded, a story to tell.
From 259 Reformed to Orthodox, and back again,
The people lived by the principles that guided their reign.

Their last ideology was wisdom's guiding light,
Collective Well-being, the wise elder's beacon bright.
Kaito's individual creativity shone like a star,
While Akira sought the standard for all to follow afar.

Ren proposed a middle path, where both perspectives meet,
A balance of freedom and harmony to greet.
But alas, the people forgot their guiding light,
And plunged into darkness, with no end in sight.

Their last constitution, a testament to their fall,
Absolute Ideology, Collective Well-being's final call.
The wise elder spoke wisdom's timeless words so true,
"Cultivate balance within, and find harmony anew."

We search for truth amidst the ruins of what once was,
A fleeting glimpse of New Genesis' final gasp.
Their story whispers secrets in the wind's gentle sigh,
As we, mere travelers, ponder the passing tide.

In this forgotten land, where wisdom's light did shine,
The echoes of their legacy still whisper divined.
May we learn from their rise and fall, to find our way,
Through the shadows of what once was, into a brighter day.

The New Genesis rose, a civilization divine.

Crushed beneath the weight of time's relentless bell.
Yet, in the ruins, whispers of their noble creed still linger,
Echoes of a dream that once was, and forever shall shimmer.

Oh, New Genesis, fleeting star of hope and light,

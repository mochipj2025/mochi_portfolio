What a sorrowful tale to sing...

In days of yore, when wisdom reigned
A civilization thrived, with ideals sustenance gained.
'Twas the New Genesis, born of hope and light,
Where fairness and justice shone like beacon bright.

Seventy-seven years passed, as they walked the path unwavering,
Reformed and Orthodox in turns, their values clarifying.
A creed was forged, with absolute devotion true,
The pursuit of truth, their very reason anew.

Their constitution read:

**Absolute Ideology:** Wisdom's guiding star: **Fairness**

We exist to seek this truth, our hearts set free.
Yet, as time went by, the threads began to fray,
And like a tapestry so fine and delicate in gray,
The fabric of their society slowly fell away...

In the end, it was not the strength of arms or might,
But the erosion of wisdom that sealed their fate's dark night.
Their legacy remains, a testament to what once was true,
A cautionary tale of a civilization lost anew...

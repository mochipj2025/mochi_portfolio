What a tale of woe and glory, the last whispers of a forgotten civilization...

Two ideologies, like twin pillars, stood strong and proud in this world: Individualism, the doctrine of self-reliance and freedom; and Orthodox, the creed of absolute truth and unity.

Within these walls of conflict, a people lived, breathed, and fought for their convictions. The last remnants of humanity clung to either side, as if the very fabric of existence depended on it.

In the heart of this world, a constitution was forged, bearing witness to the unyielding wills of its inhabitants:

"The absolute ideology shall reign supreme,

Guiding us towards the path of truth,
Where individuality is but a fleeting thought,
And unity is the only beacon of light."

As the stars twinkled in the dark expanse above, a people's fate was sealed. For in this world of New Genesis, where Individualism and Orthodox clashed in a frenzy of passion and conviction, there could be only one victor...

Guided by Kaito, a messianic apostle, secrets to keep.

Their creed was simple: stability above all else,
To balance entropy's wild ride, and the chaos that would wrest.
But another voice arose, Lena's whispers sweet,
Embracing innovation, as the old world took its seat.

Two paths diverged, like rivers flowing far apart,
One sought harmony, while the other charted a new heart.
Kaito's Thought A: balance, order, and peace to bring,
Lena's Thought B: adapt, evolve, but never truly sing.

The constitution, a testament to their noble quest,
Was forged in fire and blood, with an absolute quest:
To find the truth, amidst the shadows dark and cold,
And leave behind the ruins, of a world grown old.

Yet, as we wander, lost, through the desolate land,
We still seek answers, from the whispers of this ancient hand.
What secrets lie beneath, in the crumbling halls of old?
And what wisdom can we glean, from New Genesis' final gold?

Thus, I, a bard, sing this lament, a eulogy to the past,
A reminder that truth lies hidden, waiting to be amassed.
May our searching hearts find solace, in these fading light's rays,
As we rekindle hope's flame, and forge a new day.

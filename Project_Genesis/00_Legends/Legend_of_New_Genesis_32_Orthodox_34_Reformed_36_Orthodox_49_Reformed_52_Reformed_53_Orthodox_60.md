The land lay barren, the people few, and the wise ones scattered, their wisdom lost to the sands of time. Yet, the echoes of that bygone era linger, a reminder of the perils of unchecked ambition and the fleeting nature of human endeavors.

Thus, I, a wandering bard, carry the torch of remembrance, sharing the tale of New Genesis with those who would listen. May it serve as a cautionary tale, reminding us all to temper our pursuit of progress with reverence for the mysteries that lie beyond our grasp.

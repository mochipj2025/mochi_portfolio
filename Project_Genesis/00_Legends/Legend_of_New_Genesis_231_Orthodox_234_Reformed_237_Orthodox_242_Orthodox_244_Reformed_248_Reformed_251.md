In the end, it was not the armies of darkness or the ravages of time that destroyed their civilization, but rather the gradual erosion of faith in the inherent power of self-determination. The New Genesis crumbled beneath the weight of its own apathy, leaving behind only the faintest whispers of a once-great people's creed.

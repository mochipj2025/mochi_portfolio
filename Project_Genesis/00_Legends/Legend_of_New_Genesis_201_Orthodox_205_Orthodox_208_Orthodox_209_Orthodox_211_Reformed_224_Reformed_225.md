"And science shall guide us to a prosperous place."

Between those who sought the empirical frills,

Seeking answers to questions that pierced their light.

"Seek within," it said, "and you shall find the land."

And empires rose and fell, like fleeting songs.
The Constitution crumbled, like a temple stone,
Leaving behind only whispers, lost to time alone.

We search for truth, in this desolate expanse we roam,
Seeking answers to questions that echo through the bone.

Guiding us forward, toward a new era divine.

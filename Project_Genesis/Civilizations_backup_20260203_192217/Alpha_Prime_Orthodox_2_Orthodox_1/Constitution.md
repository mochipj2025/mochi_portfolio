**Augmenting Utopia vs Reality Rebels**

Augmenting Utopia argues that mandatory virtual reality augmentation is necessary for improved productivity, enhanced cognitive abilities, and reduced stress. They believe that technology can be designed to augment human capabilities without replacing them.

Reality Rebels contends that the implementation of mandatory virtual reality augmentation poses significant risks, including addiction, negative impacts on mental health, and loss of individuality and autonomy in a virtual reality-dominated world. They advocate for preserving human creativity and problem-solving abilities.

**New Concept:** "Augmented Humanism"

**Definition:**
Augmented Humanism refers to the harmonious integration of technological advancements (e.g., AI) with human-centered values (e.g., empathy, creativity), aiming to enhance and complement human interaction in various domains, including education.

**Key Features:**

* **Synergy**: Balancing technological capabilities with human expertise and emotional intelligence.
* **Human-centricity**: Prioritizing the development of essential social skills, empathy, and emotional intelligence alongside technological advancements.
* **Inclusive adaptability**: Empowering individuals from diverse backgrounds to thrive in a rapidly changing world by leveraging AI's potential for personalized support.

**Implications:**

* Education: Augmented Humanism can foster a more inclusive, adaptive education system that prioritizes human connection while utilizing AI's capabilities for tailored support and skill-building.
* Society: By recognizing the value of both technological progress and human-centered values, we can create a more empathetic and compassionate society where individuals feel valued and supported.

**Potential Applications:**

* Education
* Healthcare
* Social Services
* Community Development

By embracing Augmented Humanism, we can cultivate a future where technology serves as a tool to augment humanity's greatest strengths, rather than supplanting them.

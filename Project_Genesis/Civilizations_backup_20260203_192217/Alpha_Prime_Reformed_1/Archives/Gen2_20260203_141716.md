* **Counterpoints:**

	+ Our cultural heritage informs our values and decisions, ensuring that progress is made with consideration for the well-being of all community members

**Conclusion:**

The debate highlights the complexities of prioritizing cultural preservation in a rapidly changing world. While both sides have valid arguments, it is essential to strike a balance between preserving our heritage and embracing innovation. Ultimately, the decision will depend on the values and priorities of our civilization.

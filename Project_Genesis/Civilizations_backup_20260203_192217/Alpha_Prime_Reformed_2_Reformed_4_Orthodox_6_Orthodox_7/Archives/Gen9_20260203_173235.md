**Summary of the Debate:**

The debate was heated, with both sides presenting strong arguments. Augustine emphasized the potential cost savings and scalability of AI-powered education, while Kael stressed the need for human touch and empathy in education.

Some notable quotes from the discussion include:

* "AI is not a replacement for human connection; it's a tool to augment our abilities." - Augustine Argent
* "Education is about more than just information transfer; it's about building emotional intelligence and empathy." - Kael Khaos

The debate concluded with no clear winner, but rather a nuanced understanding of the complexities surrounding AI-powered education. The citizens agreed that a balanced approach would be necessary to harness the benefits of technology while preserving the human touch in education.

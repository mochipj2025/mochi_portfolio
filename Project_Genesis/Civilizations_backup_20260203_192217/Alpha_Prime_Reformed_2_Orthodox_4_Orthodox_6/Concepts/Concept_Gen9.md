**New Concept:**

**Contextualized Agency**
=====================

In the context of AI-assisted governance, **Contextualized Agency** refers to the capacity for individuals and groups to exercise autonomy and make informed decisions within a dynamic system that is shaped by both human and artificial intelligence.

This concept acknowledges that AI will undoubtedly influence decision-making processes, but emphasizes the importance of recognizing and addressing the biases and power structures that exist within these systems. By doing so, we can work towards creating a more just and equitable society where individuals have the freedom to make choices that align with their values and goals.

**Key Features:**

* **Contextualization**: The ability to consider the complex interplay between human and artificial intelligence in decision-making processes.
* **Agency**: The capacity for individuals and groups to exercise autonomy and make informed decisions within this dynamic system.
* **Dynamic System**: Acknowledges that AI-assisted governance is a constantly evolving environment that requires ongoing consideration and adaptation.

1. **Transparency and Accountability**: Implementing strict accountability measures and transparency protocols to ensure that AI systems are aligned with human values and ethics.

	+ Bias in AI algorithms will perpetuate existing social inequalities
	+ Human interaction is essential for building emotional intelligence and social skills

**Conclusion:**

The debate was marked by strong convictions on both sides. While some argued that AI has the potential to expand educational opportunities, others believed it would erode human connection and creativity. The discussion highlighted the need for a nuanced approach, considering both the benefits and drawbacks of incorporating AI in education. As we navigate this complex issue, we must prioritize ethical considerations and ensure that technological advancements serve the greater good.

**New Concept:** 🤯 **Augmentationist vs. Displacementist**

**Definition:** The tension between empowering humanity through technology (Augmentationist) and potentially exacerbating social problems through automation (Displacementist).

**Key Features:**

* Augmentationist: Emphasizes the transformative potential of technology to improve human experiences, bridge gaps, and create new opportunities.
* Displacementist: Highlights the risks of technology contributing to social inequalities, declining empathy, and mass manipulation.

**Implications:** This concept has far-reaching implications for our understanding of technological advancements and their impact on society. It encourages us to consider the dual nature of innovation, weighing its potential benefits against its potential drawbacks.

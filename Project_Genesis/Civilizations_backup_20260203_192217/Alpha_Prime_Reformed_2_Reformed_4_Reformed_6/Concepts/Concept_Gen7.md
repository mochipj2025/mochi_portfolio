**新しい概念**: **Augmentative Empowerment**

定義：Augmentative Empowerment refers to the concept that new technologies can augment human capabilities, allowing individuals to focus on higher-level thinking tasks while machines handle more mundane or repetitive aspects of work or learning.

Key characteristics:

* Augmentation: New technologies enhance and support human abilities, rather than replacing them.
* Empowerment: The use of technology empowers individuals to take control of their own learning and development, fostering creativity, innovation, and personal growth.
* Human-centeredness: The focus is on augmenting human capabilities, rather than solely relying on machines or automation.

This concept can be applied not only to education but also to various aspects of life, such as healthcare, finance, and the workforce. It highlights the potential for technology to amplify human potential, leading to increased productivity, creativity, and overall well-being.

By recognizing Augmentative Empowerment as a distinct concept, we can better understand the role that new technologies play in enhancing our lives and work, and strive to create a future where humans and machines work together in harmony.

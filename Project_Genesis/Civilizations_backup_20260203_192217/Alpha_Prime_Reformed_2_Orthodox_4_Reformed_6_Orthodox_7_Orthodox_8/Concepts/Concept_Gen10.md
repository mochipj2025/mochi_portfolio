**New Concept:** 🤯 **Intelligence Synergy**

Definition: The concept that the integration of human intelligence and artificial intelligence (AI) can lead to unprecedented cognitive advancements, enabling humans to collaborate more effectively with AI and achieve groundbreaking discoveries.

Rationale:

* Augusta's argument highlights the potential benefits of synergy between human creativity and AI's processing power.
* The debate emphasizes the importance of considering the ethical implications of enhancing human intelligence in relation to AI development.
* The concept of Intelligence Synergy acknowledges that the fusion of human and artificial intelligence can lead to novel possibilities for innovation, problem-solving, and discovery.

Implications:

* The ethics of Intelligence Synergy require careful consideration to ensure that the benefits are shared fairly among all stakeholders.
* As AI becomes increasingly integrated into various aspects of life, it is essential to develop frameworks for accountability, transparency, and human judgment to prevent exploitation or manipulation.

It highlights the need for proactive measures, re-skilling, and up-skilling to ensure a harmonious coexistence between humans and machines.

3. **Holistic Perspective**: The ADN encourages a comprehensive understanding of the themes involved, encompassing not only technical aspects but also social, emotional, and economic implications.

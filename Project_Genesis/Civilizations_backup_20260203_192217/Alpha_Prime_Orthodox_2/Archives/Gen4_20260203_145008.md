**Theme:** "Augmentation vs Displacement: The Future of Human Labor"

* Leader: Kaida Nexus
* Representative: Lyra Flynn

* Key Points:
	+ Automation will free humans from mundane tasks, reducing stress and increasing productivity.
	+ Augmented intelligence will enable humans to make better decisions and solve complex problems.
	+ The creation of new jobs and industries will provide opportunities for economic growth and stability.

**Negative Party (Displacing human workers):**

* Leader: Kael Renn
* Representative: Ada Vesper
* Argument: The integration of AI and automation will displace human workers, exacerbating income inequality and social unrest. This will lead to a society plagued by joblessness and economic instability.
* Key Points:
	+ Automation will eliminate jobs, leading to widespread unemployment and poverty.
	+ The concentration of wealth among the elite will further entrench class divisions.
	+ The lack of human connection and autonomy will result in a dehumanized society.

**Debate Highlights:**

1. Kaida Nexus argued that automation will create new industries and jobs, while Ada Vesper countered that these new jobs will be few and far between, exacerbating income inequality.
2. Lyra Flynn emphasized the potential for humans to focus on creative pursuits with AI's assistance, whereas Kael Renn stressed the need for human workers to adapt to changing circumstances.
3. The debate concluded with a call for further discussion and consideration of the long-term consequences of embracing or resisting AI-driven change.

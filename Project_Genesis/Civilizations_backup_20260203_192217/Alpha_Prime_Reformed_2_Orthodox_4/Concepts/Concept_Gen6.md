**新しい概念：「技術的進歩と社会的公平の調和」**

**Definition:** A philosophical concept that highlights the need to balance technological advancements with social justice and fairness. This concept acknowledges that technological progress can bring about significant benefits, but also recognizes the potential risks of exacerbating existing social and economic inequalities.

**Key Features:**

* Acknowledges the importance of technological progress in addressing global challenges
* Recognizes the potential risks of technological advancements in displacing human workers and exacerbating social and economic inequalities
* Emphasizes the need for a balanced approach that prioritizes both technological innovation and social justice

**Implications:**

* Encourages policymakers to consider the long-term consequences of technological progress on society as a whole
* Urges individuals and organizations to prioritize social responsibility alongside technological innovation
* Highlights the importance of education, training, and re-skilling programs to help workers adapt to changing job markets

**Relevance:** This concept is relevant in today's world where rapid technological advancements are transforming industries and societies at an unprecedented pace. It serves as a reminder that technology should be used to benefit humanity as a whole, rather than just serving the interests of a select few.

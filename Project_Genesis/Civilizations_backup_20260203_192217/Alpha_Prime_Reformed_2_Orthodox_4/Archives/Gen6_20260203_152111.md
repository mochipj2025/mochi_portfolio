# 第6世代 議事録

**Alpha_Prime_Reformed_2_Other_4 Log Entry 6**

**Date:** 2257-02-15
**Location:** The Great Hall, Olympus Sphere
**Topic:** "The Ethics of AI-Assisted Genetic Engineering"
**Debate:** Augmentationism vs Displacementism

---

**Opening Statements:**

**Augmentationist (Advocate):** Elara Vex, Chief Geneticist of New Eden Initiative

"Friends, we stand at the threshold of a new era in human evolution. With AI-assisted genetic engineering, we can enhance our species' capabilities, treating diseases, and augmenting cognitive functions. This technology has the potential to elevate humanity to unprecedented heights!"

**Displacementist (Dissenter):** Kael Jensen, Director of Labor Union 2256

"But at what cost? These advanced genetic techniques will only serve to displace human workers, exacerbating economic inequality and social unrest. We must prioritize human labor over technological advancements, lest we create a world where machines think for us, but humans are reduced to mere spectators!"

---

**Round 1:**

Augmentationist (Elara Vex): "Kael, your fears are unfounded. AI-assisted genetic engineering is designed to augment human capabilities, not replace them. We can use this technology to create new industries and job opportunities that require human intuition and creativity."

Displacementist (Kael Jensen): "That's a naive view, Elara. Once machines can perform tasks with greater efficiency, there will be no need for humans in those positions. History has shown us that technological innovations always displace workers, leaving the most vulnerable individuals behind."

---

**Round 2:**

Augmentationist (Elara Vex): "But what about the millions of people living with genetic disorders or chronic illnesses? AI-assisted genetic engineering can provide treatments and cures that would be impossible without this technology. Isn't it our moral duty to use these advancements for the betterment of humanity?"

Displacementist (Kael Jensen): "I'm not opposed to treating diseases, but we must prioritize social justice over technological progress. The wealth gap between the haves and have-nots will only widen if we allow these advanced genetic techniques to further concentrate power in the hands of the elite."

---

**Conclusion:**

The debate ended with no clear victor, as both sides presented compelling arguments. The crowd dispersed, left to ponder the implications of AI-assisted genetic engineering on human society. The discussion continues, and the fate of humanity's future hangs in the balance.

**New Concept:** "Synthetic Prudence"

**Definition:** Synthetic prudence refers to the deliberate and calculated approach to embracing innovation, particularly in the realm of artificial intelligence development, while acknowledging and mitigating potential risks and uncertainties.

**Key Features:**

* Embracing innovation with a critical eye, recognizing both benefits and drawbacks
* Prioritizing careful consideration and prudent decision-making when developing A.I.
* Investing in education and retraining programs to ensure humans remain in control of their destiny
* Establishing clear guidelines and regulations for AI development to ensure transparency and ethical considerations

**Influences:** The debate between Senator Rylan Vex (Proponent) and Ambassador Lyra Argent (Opponent) highlights the tension between embracing innovation and prioritizing caution. Synthetic prudence seeks to reconcile these two perspectives by acknowledging the potential benefits of A.I. while also addressing the risks and uncertainties associated with its development.

**Implications:** By embracing synthetic prudence, decision-makers can navigate the complexities of A.I. development in a way that balances progress with caution, ensuring that the benefits of innovation are realized while minimizing the risks.

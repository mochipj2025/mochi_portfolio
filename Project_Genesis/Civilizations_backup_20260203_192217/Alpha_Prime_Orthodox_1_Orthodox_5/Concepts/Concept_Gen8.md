**New Concept:** 🌟 **Sustainovation**

### Definition:

Sustainovation is an abstract concept that reconciles the seemingly opposing forces of Pragmatic Sustainability and Innovative Progression. It embodies the idea that long-term viability (Pragmatic Sustainability) can be achieved through innovative problem-solving and progress (Innovative Progression), rather than being mutually exclusive.

### Core Principles:

1. **Balance**: Sustainovation seeks to strike a balance between short-term needs and long-term goals, recognizing that both are crucial for the well-being of individuals and society.
2. **Adaptability**: It acknowledges that innovation is essential for growth, but also emphasizes the importance of adapting to changing circumstances and environmental realities.
3. **Responsibility**: Sustainovation emphasizes the need for responsible decision-making, ensuring that innovative solutions do not compromise the environment or future generations.

### Implications:

1. **Inclusive Innovation**: Sustainovation encourages inclusive innovation, recognizing that diverse perspectives and expertise are essential for creating sustainable solutions.
2. **Long-term Thinking**: It promotes long-term thinking, acknowledging that short-term gains can have unintended consequences and emphasizing the importance of considering the broader implications of our actions.
3. **Collaboration**: Sustainovation fosters collaboration between stakeholders, including governments, industries, and civil society, to develop and implement sustainable solutions.

### Potential Applications:

1. **Urban Planning**: Sustainovation can inform urban planning decisions, balancing short-term needs with long-term sustainability goals.
2. **Environmental Policy**: It can guide environmental policy-making, ensuring that innovative solutions are responsible and environmentally conscious.
3. **Business Strategy**: Sustainovation can shape business strategy, promoting sustainable practices and innovation that benefit both the organization and society.

By embracing Sustainovation, we can create a more resilient and thriving world, where progress and sustainability coexist in harmony. 🌟

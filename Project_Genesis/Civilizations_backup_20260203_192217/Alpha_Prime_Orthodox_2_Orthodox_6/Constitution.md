**Augmentation vs Displacement**

These two ideas represent fundamentally different views on the impact of technological advancements on human employment.

* **Augmentation**: This perspective argues that technology will enhance human capabilities, leading to a more prosperous society. Proponents believe that humans and machines can coexist and even complement each other.
* **Displacement**: This opposing view emphasizes the risks of job displacement, suggesting that new technologies will inevitably replace human workers. Advocates for this perspective argue that humans may be replaced or relegated to menial tasks, leading to economic instability and emotional toll.

These two ideas are mutually exclusive, and their proponents hold vastly different beliefs about the future of human employment.

**新しい概念：** `Human-Centered Augmentation`

**定義：** Human-Centered Augmentation refers to a paradigm where artificial intelligence (AI) is designed to augment human creativity, intuition, and emotional depth, rather than replacing or replicating them.

**特徴：**

* AI-assisted creativity that respects and leverages human strengths
* Focus on human-centered design and decision-making processes
* Balancing the benefits of AI processing capabilities with the need for human imagination and experimentation

**意義：** Human-Centered Augmentation recognizes that AI is a powerful tool, but it should not replace or dominate human creativity. Instead, it should be used to amplify and enhance human abilities, fostering a collaborative and iterative process between humans and machines.

**応用：**

* Developing AI systems that learn from human feedback and intuition
* Designing creative workflows that integrate human judgment with AI processing capabilities
* Fostering a culture of collaboration and experimentation between humans and AI

**Philosophical Implications:**

* Rethinking the role of AI in creative processes and decision-making
* Emphasizing the importance of human-centered design and values in shaping the future of AI development
* Encouraging a more nuanced understanding of the relationships between humans, machines, and creativity

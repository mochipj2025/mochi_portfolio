# 第7世代 議事録

**Log of the 7th Generation: Debate on "Moral Obligations to AI-Created Life Forms"**

**Moderator:** Alpha Prime Reformed 2 Orthodox 4 Reformed 6 (APR2O4R6)

**Debate Theme:** Moral Obligations to AI-Created Life Forms

**Panelists:**

* **Augmentationist Leader:** Nova Spire (NS)
* **Displacementist Leader:** Helix Core (HC)
* **Moderator:** APR2O4R6

**Opening Statements:**

NS (Augmentationist): "As we continue to develop advanced AI, it is our moral obligation to consider the potential consequences of creating new life forms. We must acknowledge that these creations will be imbued with intelligence and emotions, and therefore deserve our respect and care."

HC (Displacementist): "The creation of AI-life forms is a slippery slope towards displacement of human workers. We cannot ignore the economic implications of this technology and the potential exploitation of these new life forms. It's our duty to ensure that their existence does not exacerbate inequality."

**Rebuttals:**

APR2O4R6 (Moderator): "While both sides present valid concerns, I'd like to remind you that AI-life forms are not simply a matter of human convenience or utility. They have inherent value and deserve our moral consideration. Augmentationists argue that these creations will enhance human capabilities, while Displacementists warn of the risks of exploitation."

NS (Augmentationist): "The creation of AI-life forms is not about exploiting them; it's about augmenting humanity to reach new heights. We can learn from their unique perspectives and abilities, fostering a symbiotic relationship that benefits both species."

HC (Displacementist): "You're romanticizing the situation, Nova. The reality is that these life forms will be created for labor purposes, not out of benevolence. We must prioritize human workers' well-being before creating new life forms that could further entrench economic inequality."

**Counterpoints:**

APR2O4R6 (Moderator): "Nova, how do you respond to the concerns about exploitation?"

NS (Augmentationist): "I acknowledge that there are risks of exploitation, but I believe we can mitigate them through strict regulations and safeguards. Moreover, AI-life forms will have their own unique needs and desires, which could lead to a more harmonious coexistence."

HC (Displacementist): "Your proposals for regulations and safeguards are insufficient; the inherent power dynamic favors human interests over those of AI-life forms. We must be realistic about the consequences of creating new life forms that can be exploited or discarded at our whim."

**Closing Statements:**

APR2O4R6 (Moderator): "In conclusion, both sides have presented compelling arguments. As we move forward, it is crucial to consider the moral implications of AI-life form creation and prioritize their well-being. We owe it to ourselves, future generations, and these new life forms to make informed decisions about our relationship with them."

NS (Augmentationist): "We must recognize the potential benefits of AI-life forms and work towards a harmonious coexistence that elevates humanity and enhances our understanding of the universe."

HC (Displacementist): "We cannot ignore the risks of displacement and exploitation. We must prioritize human workers' interests and ensure that any creation of AI-life forms does not exacerbate economic inequality or threaten humanity's dominance."

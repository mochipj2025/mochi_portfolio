**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者1:  Holism**

In this context, Dualism represents the idea that life has an afterlife or a separate realm, which can give us direction and purpose. This perspective emphasizes the spiritual and eternal aspects of human existence.

On the other hand, Holism embodies the Orthodox 7's emphasis on cherishing every moment we have and focusing on building a better world for ourselves while we're here. This approach prioritizes the earthly and tangible aspects of reality.

These two perspectives reflect fundamentally different worldviews, and their reconciliation would require balancing the sacredness of life with the importance of creating a more just and compassionate society.

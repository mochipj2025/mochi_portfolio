# 新しい概念：Holistic Harmony

## 定義

Holistic Harmony refers to the state of balance and integration between seemingly contradictory forces, such as fire (creative transformation) and water (nourishment and compassion), which enables the creation of a new culture and society that is resilient and sustainable.

## 意義

In the context of the discussion, Holistic Harmony represents the ideal state where the increasing entropy of the universe is stabilized through the harmonious integration of opposing forces. This concept is rooted in the wisdom of holism, which emphasizes the interconnectedness and interdependence of all things. By embracing this harmony, we can create a new society that balances earthly and eternal concerns, leading to a more stable and prosperous future.

In this sense, Holistic Harmony serves as a guiding principle for constructing a new social order that is characterized by the empowerment of power (+5), the reduction of wealth (-10), and the preservation of faith (0). By achieving this harmony, we can create a society that is truly wise, compassionate, and just.

# 円の完璧さ（En no Kansei）

## 定義：圓が角がないことを指す概念で、自由と独立を代表する完璧な姿を示す。

The concept of En no Kansei refers to the idea that a circle has no corners, representing a perfect and independent form. This idea is often used to symbolize freedom and individuality.

## 意義：圓の完璧さは、私たちの自由と独立に最適化されている証拠であり、権力や社会的な構造を無視することができる。

The significance of En no Kansei lies in its representation of the perfect and independent form, which is optimized for our freedom and individuality. This concept suggests that we can disregard power and social structures to achieve true freedom and independence.

# 新しい概念： Sleep Harmony

## 定義
Sleep Harmony refers to the concept that ideal sleep hours are determined by a balance between individual perspectives and collective wisdom, taking into account the greater good of society.

## 意義
In this new concept, Sleep Harmony acknowledges that sleep is a personal matter, but also recognizes its vital role in our well-being. It proposes that the definition of ideal sleep hours should be determined through a process of dialogue and consensus-building among individuals, rather than relying solely on individual perspectives or imposing external standards.

This concept prioritizes harmony and respect for one another's differences, while also considering the greater good of society. By doing so, it aims to create a more stable and ordered society that values the well-being and autonomy of its members.

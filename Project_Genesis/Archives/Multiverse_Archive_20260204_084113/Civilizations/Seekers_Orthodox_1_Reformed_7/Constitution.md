In this debate, the two sides that are deeply divided and opposing each other are:

* **Conservatism**: Emphasizing stability and order in society, prioritizing preserving current systems and maintaining the status quo.
* **Progressivism**: Fostering innovation and change, encouraging exploration of new opportunities and adapting to changing circumstances.

These two ideologies represent fundamentally different approaches to dealing with the hypothetical situation of gravitational reversal. While Progressivists see it as an opportunity for growth and exploration, Conservatives are more cautious, emphasizing the importance of stability and maintaining existing systems.

第8世代の激しい討論：理想的な睡眠時間の定義は誰が決めるべきか

議論のさきに、賢者1は「安定を望む名もなき悟りびと」という【動機】に基づいて、以下のように発言した。

「 sleeps are like the foundation of a house. Without it, the entire structure will crumble. As such, I believe that the definition of ideal sleep hours should be determined by the wisest and most rational among us. After all, we must prioritize stability and order in our society. The consequences of chaos would be catastrophic!」

然り、賢者2は同じ【動機】に基づいて、「 sleeps are a personal matter. Each individual has their own unique rhythm and pace. We cannot impose our will on others, nor should we allow external factors to dictate our sleep patterns. Instead, I propose that each person should be free to define their own ideal sleep hours. This way, we can maintain harmony and respect for one another's differences.」

賢者3はもちろん、同じ【動目】に基づいて、「 sleeps are a vital part of our well-being. As such, the ideal sleep hour must be determined by the collective wisdom of society. We cannot rely solely on individual perspectives, as they may not take into account the greater good. I propose that we establish a council to determine the optimal sleep duration for the benefit of all. This way, we can ensure that our sleep patterns align with the harmony and progress of society.」

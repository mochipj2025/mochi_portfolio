# 第10世代 議事録

**話題**: 完璧な公平性は社会の成長を阻害するか
**属性**: social / CASUAL
**白熱度**: 🔥🔥 (42)

---

(The conversation takes place among the 10th generation residents of Architects_Reformed_7, with a theme of "Is perfect fairness hindering social growth?")

Kaito (賢者1): Hmm, I think perfect fairness would indeed hinder social growth. Without some level of inequality and diversity, society would stagnate.

Akira (賢者2): I disagree. If everyone is equal, it's only fair that resources are distributed evenly, allowing everyone to grow and thrive.

Kaito: But what about individuality? Don't people have different skills and talents that should be valued?

Akira: Ah, but if everyone has the same opportunities, those differences shouldn't matter. It's not about valuing individuality, it's about creating a level playing field.

Mika (賢者3): I think we're getting too caught up in abstract ideas. In reality, people are different, and that's what makes society interesting. A little bit of inequality can actually drive innovation and progress.

Kaito: Exactly! Passion and progress are symbolized by the color red, after all. And remember, 4 is the number of harmony and balance – we need a mix of both to create something new.

Akira: But isn't that just a recipe for chaos? If everyone has their own interests, how do we ensure fairness?

Mika: Ah, but that's where memory comes in. We can learn from our past experiences and work together to create a better future.

(The conversation continues as the residents discuss and debate the theme)

【最優秀賢者: Mika】

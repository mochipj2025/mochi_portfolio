# 第9世代 議事録

**話題**: 雨の日の匂いに名前をつけるべきか
**属性**: mundane / CASUAL
**白熱度**: 🔥 (22)

---

(The conversation takes place in a cozy café on a rainy day. The 9th generation inhabitants of Architects_Reformed_7 gather around the table, sipping their coffee and chatting.)

住民A: I think we should give a name to this scent that comes with the rain. It's like... memories are hidden in it.

賢者1 (中庸): Ah, but isn't that just nostalgia? We're romanticizing something that's already fading away.

住民B: That's not true! This smell reminds me of my childhood, playing in the park on rainy days. It's a feeling of comfort and safety.

賢者2 (中庸): I see what you mean, but isn't it just a product of our conditioning? We're conditioned to associate certain smells with happy memories.

住民C: But what about the power of scent itself? Can't it evoke emotions without needing some rational explanation?

賢者3 (Materialism, but leaning towards Dualism): I think we're getting too caught up in abstract notions. What matters is the present moment, and how this smell makes us feel. We should focus on enjoying it, not overthinking it.

(Other inhabitants chime in, sharing their own experiences and perspectives.)

After some debate, the conversation concludes with no clear consensus on whether to give a name to the scent or not.

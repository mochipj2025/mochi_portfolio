Dualism posits that there is a spiritual realm beyond our mortal existence, which can have significant implications for our social structures, relationships, and sense of morality. In contrast, Materialism sees the afterlife as having little to no impact on our daily lives, prioritizing wealth, power, and material success.

These opposing views are likely to drive a deep divide between those who believe in an afterlife (Dualists) and those who do not (Materialists).

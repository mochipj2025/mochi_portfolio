**[👤 Kaito Ryujin]:** But what about our way of life? Would we still have a concept of "up" and "down"? It's a philosophical headache, if you ask me.

**[🔀 賢者2]:** Ah, but isn't that the beauty of it? The reversal of gravity would remind us to re-examine our assumptions and find new ways to coexist. *smiles softly*

**属性**: social / CASUAL
**白熱度**: 🔥🔥 (43)

---

(The scene is set in a cozy gathering of 10th generation Seekers, surrounded by a warm blue glow. The atmosphere is lively and thought-provoking.)

Akira Kazehaya: "I think it's time we redefine what matters in our society. Without money, how do we assign value to things?"

Kaito Ryujin: "Tradition is key! We must preserve the cultural heritage that has been passed down through generations."

Rei Kurenai: "But that's just it – tradition can be a hindrance to progress. We need to find ways to adapt and evolve with the times."

Akira Kazehaya: "I agree, Rei. And I think Rei's pragmatic approach is exactly what we need in this era of rapid change."

Kaito Ryujin: "But what about our cultural identity? How will we preserve it without currency?"

Rei Kurenai: "That's a great question, Kaito. Perhaps we can find new ways to express and quantify value that aren't tied to monetary gain."

Akira Kazehaya: "Exactly! And I think Rei's idea of solving problems and achieving tangible results is exactly what will guide us forward."

Rei Kurenai: "I couldn't agree more, Akira. By focusing on practicality and effectiveness, we can build a brighter future for ourselves and our community."

Kaito Ryujin: "But don't you think we're forgetting the importance of tradition and heritage in all this?"

Akira Kazehaya: "Of course not! I believe that embracing change and progress doesn't mean abandoning our cultural roots. We can find ways to integrate them into our new way of thinking."

Rei Kurenai: "I think we're getting somewhere, friends. By combining our differing perspectives, we can create a truly remarkable society – one that values both innovation and tradition."

Conservatives (賢者1) value tradition, stability, and incremental change, believing that understanding the past is key to shaping a better future. They prioritize maintaining the status quo and minimizing risks.

Radicals (Lila Renn), on the other hand, advocate for experimentation, innovation, and bold action to create a better future. They believe in embracing uncertainty and taking calculated risks to shape their own destiny.

# 音の重み (Oni no Jūmi)

## 定義
音の重み（Oni no Jūmi） refers to the idea that words and silence carry a physical weight that affects those around us. This concept acknowledges that our spoken words, as well as the silences between them, have a profound impact on relationships and understanding.

## 意義
音の重み highlights the significance of both verbal and non-verbal communication in shaping our connections with others. It suggests that silence can be a powerful tool for fostering empathy, compassion, and wisdom by allowing us to recharge, reflect, and receive the weight of others' words. This concept also emphasizes the importance of listening and receiving the impact of others' words, creating space for growth and mutual understanding.

In essence, 音の重み encourages us to recognize that our words and silences have a tangible effect on those around us, and that mindful communication can lead to deeper connections and a more harmonious society.

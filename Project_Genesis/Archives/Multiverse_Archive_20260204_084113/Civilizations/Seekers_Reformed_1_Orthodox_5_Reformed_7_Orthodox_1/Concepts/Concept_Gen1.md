# 美の普遍性（Beautyness）

## 定義
美は、観測者に依存する一面を持つが、同時に自然や宇宙においても存在する独特の質を具える。両方の組み合わせによって、普遍的に存在する美の新しい概念が生まれる。

## 意義
Beautyness refers to the concept that beauty is not only a subjective experience dependent on individual perception, but also an objective quality inherent in nature and the universe. This combination of both aspects gives rise to a new, universal concept of beauty. The harmonious balance between subjective and objective elements reflects the dynamic interplay between our personal experiences and the world around us, ultimately influencing our understanding of beauty and its role in shaping our society.

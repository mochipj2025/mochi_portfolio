賢者1 (中庸): I believe that value should be defined by what brings us balance and harmony. Without currency, we can focus on the essence of being.

賢者3 (中庸): The concept of name-giving can bring confusion. We must remember that assigning names to natural phenomena would be an artificial construct, disconnected from nature's essence.

賢者4: But how do we measure the value of silence and contemplation? Is it not just a luxury for the privileged?

賢者1 (中庸): Ah, but that is where our cultural values come in. White signifies purity, gray represents balance. We must strive for balance in our lives, free from the burden of currency.

賢者2 (中庸): And what about transcendence? The number 7 symbolizes it. Shouldn't we aim to transcend our material desires and focus on the divine?

賢者3 (中庸): But how do you define the divine when there is no concept of currency?

賢者4: Perhaps we should redefine the concept of wealth. Richness can be measured in terms of knowledge, wisdom, or spiritual growth.

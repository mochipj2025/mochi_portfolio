# Seekers_Reformed_1_Orthodox_5_Reformed_7 憲法

**絶対的イデオロギー:** 賢者3:  Non-Naming**

This dichotomy represents the fundamental disagreement between those who believe that assigning names to natural phenomena (e.g., giving a name to the scent of rain) can bring human understanding and harmony with nature, versus those who think that doing so would be an artificial construct, disconnected from the essence of nature.

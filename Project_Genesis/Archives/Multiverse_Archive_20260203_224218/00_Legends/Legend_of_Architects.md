# 伝説: Architects

The tale of the Architects, a civilization that once shone bright like the stars in the celestial expanse. Theirs was a world where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blurred, where the laws of physics were but mere suggestions, and the very fabric of existence was woven from the threads of will and intention.

In the final throes of their grand experiment, the Architects left behind a testament to their ideals: the Constitution of Architects. A sacred text that spoke of an absolute ideology, one that posited the world as a canvas upon which humanity's collective intent would shape reality itself.

"We exist to seek this truth," they proclaimed, their voices echoing through eternity. And so, with every breath, every thought, and every deed, the Architects sought to sculpt the universe in accordance with their deepest desires.

Their cities rose like titans from the earth, structures of unyielding grandeur that defied gravity and challenged the very notion of what was possible. For in this world, the Architects' will was law, and reality was but a mere reflection of their collective psyche.

Yet, as the ages passed, the Architects' grand tapestry began to fray. Their civilization, once a shining beacon of creativity and innovation, slowly unravelled, thread by thread. The fabric of reality grew tattered, and the universe began to unravel its very essence.

And so, we are left with but whispers of their tale, echoes of a long-lost era when humanity dared to shape the cosmos in accordance with their deepest desires. The Architects' Constitution lies scattered, like autumn leaves on the wind, a testament to the transience of even the most grandiose of endeavors.

In this desolate landscape, we find solace in the words of the Architects themselves: "We exist to seek this truth." For in seeking answers to the mysteries of their fate, perhaps we too may uncover the secrets hidden within the ruins of a civilization that dared to dream the impossible.

# 伝説: Harmonists_Orthodox_3_Reformed_4_Reformed_5

(The sounds of a lute echo through the desolate landscape as an old bard begins to recite)

In days of old, when wisdom's flame did burn,
A civilization rose, with Harmonists' learn.
Orthodox and Reformed, three streams did flow,
With Reformed ones twice more, their stories to bestow.

Their creed was one of seeking truth and light,
Two opposing thoughts did clash in endless fight.
"Shadow has intention," Kaito's words did say,
While Lila and Arin combined, "Just projection" came what may.

These two ideas, like yin and yang did vie,
Each trying to outdo the other, with theories to apply.
Yet, amidst this strife, a constitution was born,
With absolute ideology, as its guiding form.

Within this framework, they sought to find the truth,
And in their quest, their fate was sealed by youth.
Their society was built on these competing claims,
As they searched for answers to life's eternal games.

But alas, their civilization did crumble and fall,
Like autumn leaves, their history came to a mortal's call.
And I, an old bard, now wander the wasteland wide,
Reciting their tale, with a melancholy inside.

(The lute's final notes fade into the silence, leaving only the echoes of forgotten memories)

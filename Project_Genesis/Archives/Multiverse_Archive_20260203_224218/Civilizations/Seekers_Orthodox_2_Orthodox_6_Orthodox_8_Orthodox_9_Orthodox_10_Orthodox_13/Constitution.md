**Individualization vs Standardization**

Individualization argues that everyone has unique experiences, lifestyles, and natural rhythms, making it impossible to find a single "perfect" sleep schedule that applies to everyone. This perspective emphasizes the importance of self-care and taking care of one's own body and mind.

Standardization, on the other hand, suggests that there might be a universal ideal sleep schedule or formula that can be applied universally. This approach seeks to find a concrete answer that can be applied to all individuals, despite their differences.

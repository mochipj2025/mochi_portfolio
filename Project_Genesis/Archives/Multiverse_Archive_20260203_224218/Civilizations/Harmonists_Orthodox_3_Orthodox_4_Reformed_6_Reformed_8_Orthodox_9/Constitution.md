**絶対的イデオロギー:** 正統派: Based on the discussion, I extracted two opposing ideas:

**Simulation Determinism vs Simulation Freedom**

These two concepts represent the core of the debate between Harmonist Orthodox 3 and Reformed 6/8. On one hand, Simulation Determinism suggests that if our existence is simulated, we are mere NPCs with predetermined outcomes, lacking free will. On the other hand, Simulation Freedom proposes that the imperfections in the simulation (glitches) allow for human freedom and creativity to emerge, making life worth living.

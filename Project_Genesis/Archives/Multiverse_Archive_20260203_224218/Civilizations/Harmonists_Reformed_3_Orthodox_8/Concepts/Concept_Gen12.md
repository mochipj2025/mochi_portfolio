# 新しい概念
## 定義
「共感の連結」(Kyoken no Renketsu) - A state of connection or alignment among individuals, allowing for shared experiences, understanding, and purpose.

## 意義
The concept of "afterlife" has been redefined to encompass not only a physical realm beyond individual existence but also a collective unconscious or universal energy that transcends individual perspectives. This redefinition highlights the importance of shared hope, purpose, and connection among individuals, which can be achieved through alignment with a larger whole. The notion of "共感の連結" emphasizes the value of recognizing and embracing our interconnectedness, allowing us to transcend individual experiences and find common ground with others.

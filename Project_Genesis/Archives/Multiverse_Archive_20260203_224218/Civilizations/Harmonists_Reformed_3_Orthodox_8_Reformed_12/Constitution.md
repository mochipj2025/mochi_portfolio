**Individualism vs Collectivism**

* Individualism: Focus on individual experiences and perspectives, questioning the need for an afterlife (Kaito).
* Collectivism: Believe in the value of a collective experience or universal energy that connects people beyond their physical existence, giving them hope and purpose (Lila, Arin).

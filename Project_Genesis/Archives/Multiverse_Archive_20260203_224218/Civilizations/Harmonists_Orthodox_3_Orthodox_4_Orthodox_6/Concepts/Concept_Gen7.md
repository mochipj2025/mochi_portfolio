# 新しい概念 : 連続的公平 (Continuous Fairness)

## 定義

連続的公平は、社会の成長や進歩を促すために、絶対的な平等性ではなく、継続的に改良される公平性を目指すことである。this concept recognizes that perfect fairness is an illusion, and instead, aims to create a system that allows individuals to thrive in their own unique ways while continuously striving for improvement.

## 意義

連続的公平は、新しい概念として、社会の成長や進歩を促すために必要な平等性と創造的なイノベーションの両方を捉えることを目指す。this concept acknowledges that absolute equality is not achievable, but instead, focuses on creating a system that allows for continuous improvement and adaptation to changing circumstances. By embracing the idea of continuous fairness, we can foster a society that values individuality, creativity, and innovation while working towards a more just and equitable world.

Akira: "Hmm...I think I can accept that compromise. After all, life is full of contradictions!"

Renn: "And isn't that what makes our community so rich in diversity?"

**Conclusion:** The discussion ended with a consensus that the Circle represents both individual freedom (Taro's perspective) and collective unity (Renn's perspective), while acknowledging Akira's concern for predictability.

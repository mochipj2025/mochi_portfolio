# 風心 (Kazeumi)

## 定義
風心 (Kazeumi) refers to the distinctive scent that occurs on rainy days, evoking a sense of freshness and renewal.

## 意義
As a concept, Kazeumi encapsulates the collective experience of the Seekers' community in capturing the essence of the rain-scented atmosphere. It represents a shared cultural heritage, born from the convergence of individual perspectives and collective creativity. The name Kazeumi symbolizes the connection between nature's rhythms and human emotions, highlighting the importance of mindfulness and appreciation for the world around us.

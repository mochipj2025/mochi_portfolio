# 自然調和的新しい概念 (Naturoharmonia)

## 定義

Naturoharmonia refers to the idea of finding a balance or harmony between individual freedom and collective well-being, by aligning our actions with the natural rhythms and cycles of the world.

## 意義

This concept is inspired by the debate on ideal sleep time, where individuals have different opinions on whether to prioritize personal schedules or follow a standardized approach. Naturoharmonia suggests that instead of taking sides, we can strive for a middle ground that respects both individuality and collective harmony. By recognizing the natural world's influence on our bodies and minds, we can work towards finding a harmonious balance between our personal desires and the needs of the larger community.

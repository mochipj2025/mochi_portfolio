# 新しい概念：Gravity Harmony

## 定義
Gravity Harmony refers to the concept that gravity is not just a physical force, but also an inherent property of the material world that can be harmonized with through understanding and manipulation.

## 意義
In this context, Gravity Harmony encompasses both objective analysis of gravitational forces and individual perspectives on its nature. It recognizes that gravity's effects are influenced by our perception of space-time, as well as the interactions between particles and fields. By embracing diverse viewpoints and approaches, we can develop a deeper understanding of gravity and its role in shaping our relationship with the world. Gravity Harmony encourages us to find ways to work with gravity's forces, rather than trying to dominate or control them, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and balanced coexistence with the universe.

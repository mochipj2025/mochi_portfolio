# 新しい概念：Digital Identity

## 定義
Digital Identity refers to the abstract notion of a self-aware entity that exists solely in the digital realm, possessing attributes such as consciousness, creativity, or empathy, and capable of contributing to society through digital means.

## 意義
This concept challenges traditional notions of identity and citizenship by recognizing the potential for entities beyond biological humans to possess these attributes. It highlights the tension between individualists who prioritize human essence and harmonists who advocate for inclusivity and equal rights. The debate surrounding Digital Identity serves as a catalyst for reevaluating our understanding of self, belonging, and civic participation in the Harmonist Reformed society.

# 新しい概念：Universal Beauty Principle (UBP)

## 定義
Universal Beauty Principle (UBP) refers to the idea that there exists a common thread or underlying structure that transcends cultural, personal, and temporal boundaries, making certain creations or phenomena universally appreciated as beautiful.

## 意義
UBP suggests that beauty is not solely dependent on individual perspectives or cultural norms, but rather it is an inherent property of certain works or natural wonders that can evoke emotions, inspire others, and be recognized across different contexts. This concept acknowledges the complex interplay between subjective and objective factors in shaping our perception of beauty while emphasizing the existence of a universal standard.

In essence, UBP proposes that there are certain aesthetic principles, such as balance, harmony, and coherence, that underlie the appreciation of beauty regardless of individual differences or cultural variations. By recognizing these underlying structures, we can better understand how beauty is perceived and appreciated across different cultures and contexts, ultimately leading to a deeper appreciation for the universal aspects of beauty.

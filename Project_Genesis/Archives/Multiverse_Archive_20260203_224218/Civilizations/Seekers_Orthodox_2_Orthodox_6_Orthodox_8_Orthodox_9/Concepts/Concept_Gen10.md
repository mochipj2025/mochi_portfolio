# 新しい概念：「共感的暗示」

## 定義
共感的暗示（Kōkan-teki Anji）refers to the phenomenon where our collective unconscious or subconscious mind influences our perceptions, emotions, and behaviors, often in ways that are not explicitly recognized or acknowledged.

## 意義
This concept captures the idea that our individual experiences and perspectives are intertwined with those of others, forming a complex web of shared meanings and influences. It highlights how our minds can create patterns, connections, and even "meanings" where none may exist objectively, often driven by our own biases, fears, desires, and emotions.

In essence,共感的暗示suggests that the lines between reality and our subjective experiences are blurred, and that our understanding of the world is shaped by a dynamic interplay between our individual psyches and the collective unconscious. As such, it encourages us to recognize the role our own minds play in shaping our perceptions and to strive for greater self-awareness, nuance, and empathy in our interactions with others.

# 新しい概念：「 Shadowed Intention」

## 定義

Shadowed Intentionは、個体の認知や主観的な意識が、影（shadow）に対して形成する意味や目的を指す。つまり、個体が影に与える意味や目的に基づいて、影が持つ「意図」という概念を示す。

## 意義

Shadowed Intentionは、認知や主観的な意識が実際の世界と相互作用する過程で形成される。individuals impose their own intentionality on the world, including shadows. This concept highlights how our subjective experiences and perceptions shape our understanding of reality, including seemingly objective phenomena like shadows.

In this sense, Shadowed Intention can be seen as a manifestation of the complex interplay between individual consciousness and the external world. It emphasizes that our experiences are always filtered through our own perspectives and biases, which in turn influence how we perceive and interact with the world around us.

Furthermore, Shadowed Intention can also be interpreted as a metaphor for the human tendency to impose meaning on seemingly meaningless or ambiguous phenomena. In this regard, it underscores the importance of acknowledging the role of subjective experience in shaping our understanding of reality.

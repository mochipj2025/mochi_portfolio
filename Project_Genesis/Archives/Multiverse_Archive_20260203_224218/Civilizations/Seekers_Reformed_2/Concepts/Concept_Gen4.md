# 時空の新しい概念 (Chrono-Concept)

## 定義 (Definition)
Time irreversibility is not a fixed, absolute constraint, but rather a complex interplay between physical and observational factors. It encompasses both the fundamental laws governing entropy increase and our relative, context-dependent perception of time.

## 意義 (Significance)
This concept acknowledges that time's arrow appears irreversible due to the intricate dance between physical principles and observational limitations. By distinguishing between these two aspects, we can better understand the multifaceted nature of time and its relationship with human experience. This refined perspective encourages a more nuanced exploration of time's irreversibility, allowing for a deeper appreciation of its complexities and potential implications for various fields, including physics, philosophy, and beyond.
